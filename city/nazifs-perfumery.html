Nazif’s Perfumery

Nazif’s Perfumery is a dimly lit establishment in the Grand Bazaar, known for the muted musk of various fragrances that permeate the air. The shop previously served as a hub for access to the undercity, managed by Nazif, a perfumist who was recently killed during the Festival of Candles. It features shelves lined with vials and jars containing scents, creating a subdued atmosphere.

The perfumery is currently under the control of a black cat named Sharif the Strong Fur, who now oversees operations in exchange for coin or a password. Sharif maintains a no-magic rule due to the volatile circumstances following the festival. He has claimed authority over the perfumery and has requested assistance for four gravely ill cats. Sharif noted that Nazif had been relieved of his duties by the Ghost of March and asserted that all cats report to him, referencing a local folk tale about cats defeating monkeys in Istanbul. This emphasizes the perfumery's role as a critical access point to the undercity amid shifting power dynamics following Nazif's assassination.
