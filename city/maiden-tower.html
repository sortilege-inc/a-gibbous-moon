Maiden Tower

The Maiden Tower is situated beneath the Basilica Cistern. It is linked to the Crown of Sargon, a powerful item associated with a faceless ghost. The tower is noted as a significant location for retrieving this item. The Blue Rider, connected to the races, stated that the Crown of Sargon could be accessed below the Maiden Tower.

The location of the Maiden Tower was shared with a friendly cat near Papa Mert’s, indicating an intention to pursue the Crown of Sargon.
