Laboratory

The Laboratory is a secured area within an underground complex, defined by a large desk cluttered with broken vials and petrified herbs. Most of the liquids are destroyed, but one intact bottle contains a viscous black substance awaiting potential identification. The Laboratory shows signs of past alchemical or magical experimentation through the remnants present.

The atmosphere is somber, suggesting abandonment. The Laboratory reflects remnants of past endeavors in magic or alchemy and hints at the dangers that may lurk in the surrounding areas.
