Arraf Tavern

Arraf Tavern is located in the Undercity, accessible through Nazif’s Scents in the Grand Bazaar. It features a stage where a human-presenting singer named Kore performs, often decorated with flowers. The interior is adorned with carpets and attracts a diverse clientele, including tieflings, half-orcs, and half-elves, reflecting the cultural diversity of the Undercity. Patrons engage in discussions about faith and inclusion while enjoying food and drink, including fresh bread and stew.

Arraf Tavern serves as a meeting point for individuals seeking community and understanding, as indicated by the conversations regarding faith and inclusion for non-humans. It acts as a social hub for those who may not have access to the upper city, where discussions about the political climate and the integration of different communities take place. The focus here is on safety and freedom of movement while preserving Undercity spaces. The lively atmosphere is enriched by a mix of languages and dialects among the patrons.

Kore, the singer associated with Arraf Tavern, provides introductions and insights about local dynamics. She occasionally refers to Alemdar but clarifies that there is no formal patronage tie.
