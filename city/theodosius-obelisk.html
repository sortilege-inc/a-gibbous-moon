Theodosius Obelisk

The Theodosius Obelisk is a prominent structure located near the Hagia Sophia, serving as a notable landmark. It functions as a gathering point for events, including magical horse races that attract a diverse crowd. The obelisk is characterized by its sundial-like appearance, contributing to the unique atmosphere of the plaza.

The obelisk is set to host an upcoming horse race, which is distinguished by its underground nature. The horses involved in the race are magical, adding an element of intrigue to the occasion. The obelisk's location and significance as a meeting point for various activities highlight its role in the social and cultural fabric of the surrounding area.
