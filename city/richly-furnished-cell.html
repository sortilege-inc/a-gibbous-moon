richly furnished cell

The cell is a richly furnished chamber featuring rugs on the floor and ceiling, chairs, tables, a reading table, and a glass of drink. A young woman in a silk green dress occupies the space. Observers can peer through a viewing opening into the cell. The woman, identified as Mahidevran Hatun, is Suleiman’s favorite concubine. She mentions having been secluded for a few days for her safety and inquires whether Suleiman is ready for her.
