Sif's Books & Manuscripts

Sif's Books & Manuscripts is a rare bookshop known for its eclectic collection. The shop features carefully stacked books that overflow from the shelves, creating a cozy yet cluttered atmosphere. The proprietor is a tall tiefling who initially presents as curt but engages in conversation, showing a willingness to negotiate on the sale of rare tomes. The environment conveys that the books are valuable and well cared for, despite the limited space.

Jean-Zera Mongé visited Sips and Florida Sea Light seeking a buyer for a tome taken from Osman. Upon entering, Jean-Zera observed the shop's unique charm and approached the tiefling proprietor. After an initial curt exchange, the tiefling invited Jean-Zera to discuss the potential sale, leading to a negotiation regarding the book. During this interaction, Jean-Zera examined a recovered spellbook and identified several spells within it, including Parquet Lock, Mantle of the Sophists Avicenna, Solomon’s Everlasting Wrath, and Conjure Animals.
