Pera Palace Hotel

The Pera Palace Hotel is located above ground and serves as a place of refuge and healing. It is associated with Dr. Feuerbach, who provides medical assistance for ailments such as blood rot. The hotel is a location where individuals can seek help, as demonstrated by Shehrazat, a young tiefling suffering from a magical affliction, who was advised to seek treatment there.

The hotel is linked to the upper echelons of society, referenced in an invitation from Abd-ul Mejid, indicating its significance in the political landscape. The Pera Palace Hotel is characterized by its role in providing care and support to those in need, particularly regarding magical ailments.
