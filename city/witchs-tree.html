Witch’s Tree

Invisible Attacker's Tree is a large tree located two hills from a Cemetery, roughly two and a half times the size of the surrounding trees. It is significant in local folklore, associated with a witch and causing disturbances for nearby Scavengers—mischievous fae creatures that once inhabited the tree. The area around the tree is dark and overgrown, creating a sense of mystery and danger.

Invisible Attacker's Tree has been the site of hostility during night investigations. Sidra al-Najjar and Ahmet Yusuf were confronted with rocks thrown from the tree's direction, resulting in injury. In retaliation, Sidra cast a Fire Bolt, igniting the upper branches and illuminating the area, which revealed an invisible, spindly creature moving among the branches. After a series of confrontations, the creature was defeated, and the fire was extinguished. Subsequently, Sidra and Ahmet reassured the nearby Scavengers that the tree was safe, leading to celebrations among them.
