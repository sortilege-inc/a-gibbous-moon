Banished Angels

The Banished Angels is a clandestine stronghold located in the Kasimpasha District, known for its seedy reputation. The entrance is marked by faint Arabic letters on a plain door, situated in a narrow alley surrounded by various shops. Inside, the structure features long hallways with multiple doors, one leading to an armory filled with an extensive collection of weapons. The interior is dimly lit by torches and a gas lantern, with stone steps descending deeper into the facility.

The stronghold serves as a meeting point for the remnants of the Janissaries, led by Ahra “the Wolf,” who is engaged in rebuilding their strength while navigating the underworld of Constantinople.
