Styx Bathhouse

The Styx Bathhouse is a prominent establishment in the Undercity, serving as a hub for relaxation and social interaction among a diverse clientele. It features elaborate brickwork and a spacious layout that includes a large central pool surrounded by several smaller pools situated off to the sides. The design incorporates Roman and Turkish influences, creating an open area for patrons to mingle and relax. Private rooms are available for discreet interactions, making it an ideal venue for gathering local rumors and conducting business deals. The bathhouse attracts both upper-class visitors and residents from various backgrounds, including non-human folk. It is located within the vibrant arts district known as the Leaky Pit, characterized by numerous venues and a lively atmosphere.

Recently, patrons at the Styx Bathhouse have been notably reserved about certain topics, particularly concerning the activities of the Janissaries and the Ghost of March. Conversations within the bathhouse remain subdued, despite its role as a gathering spot for gossip and news. The facility is operated by Hussein, who facilitates conversations and business dealings among visitors. The bathhouse is situated near the Thieves Bazaar and is connected to various underground routes, making it a strategic location for those navigating the Undercity.
