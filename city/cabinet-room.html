Cabinet Room

The Cabinet Room holds a number of objects of historical moment — three rings of value, an amulet, and several desk-boxes containing correspondence between Mehmed II and Vlad Tepes upon the rule of Wallachia and the matter of hostages held at the Ottoman court.

Hard by lies a secured Armory, well stocked with weapons and other objects of price — so that the room serves a double purpose, at once a repository of history and a cache for those who go adventuring.
