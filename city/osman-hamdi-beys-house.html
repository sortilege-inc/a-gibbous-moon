Osman Hamdi Bey’s House

Osman Hamdi Bey’s House features a library that has been selectively looted, with notable items missing, including Osman's Journal and several books on ciphers, charm magic, and archaeological digs. Osman has not returned home, and the library's security is managed by Ashraf, a tortoise, while the Imperial Guard is stationed outside. The house contains secret compartments and an artifact vault, as noted in a letter addressed to his friends.

A secret compartment was discovered in Osman’s desk, containing a folded, sealed letter bearing his seal. Osman's Journal was found hidden behind an open book held by a bust in the library. The letter implied that Osman was likely dead and bequeathed his manor to the party, stating that his journal contains the location of the Padishah’s Consort’s sister, a powerful diviner. The letter also mentioned that the journal is locked both mundanely and arcane, and provided a clue to seek help from an ally to decrypt it.
