Bathhouse Safehouse

The Bathhouse Safehouse is a concealed refuge located underground, primarily used for stashing valuable items and providing shelter after dangerous encounters. It includes secure areas for safe storage, allowing users to keep their belongings hidden from prying eyes and providing a sense of security. The climate is damp and cool, typical of subterranean environments, which aids in preserving stored items. The structure features various chambers, including a main area for gathering and smaller rooms for storage and exploration.

The safehouse has been utilized after encounters with undead threats, including a Vampire. Users store significant loot here to avoid detection and ensure the safety of their findings. Among the items typically secured are valuable artifacts and weapons, such as a Necrotic Short Sword and plate mail. The safehouse serves as a key location before meeting valuable contacts in the area.
