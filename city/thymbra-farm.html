Thymbra Farm

Thymbra Farm, a serene estate nestled in the gently rolling hills north of Constantinople, serves as the residence of the Calvert family. This picturesque farmhouse, surrounded by vibrant fields, lush trees, and blooming flowers, has become a favored venue for social gatherings hosted by Frederick and Evelyn Calvert. Presently, the farm is experiencing a lull in activity as many workers from a nearby archaeological dig, uncovering an ancient Greek site, have taken leave following a recent festival.

The tranquility of Thymbra Farm is occasionally interrupted by notable guests, such as the royal polymath Osman Hamdi Bey, French General Patrice de MacMahon, and the archaeological enthusiasts Heinrich and Sophia Schliemannn. Recent dinner conversations brimmed with a spectrum of languages, primarily French, alongside Turkish and English, as guests shared insights about the promising findings from the excavation, including a megaron. Children, including young Sidra al-Najjar and Ahmet Yusuf, received commendations for their contributions to the dig, while the evening’s entertainment was provided by Jean-Zera Mongé.

However, the peace of Thymbra Farm was shattered when a mysterious crack echoed through the morning air, causing a nearby flowering tree to experience a striking transformation—a 30-foot radius around it drained of color, while the tree itself remained alive despite this bizarre phenomenon. Ahmet Yusuf, curious and observant, ventured closer, as concerns began to mount among the farmhouse inhabitants regarding the unexplained absence of Heinrich Schliemann and Frank Calvert.

The investigation led to the discovery of a narrow crevice nearby, opening into a cave rich with archaeological treasures. This intimate connection between the natural landscape and the artifacts of the past has made Thymbra Farm not only a place of comfort and camaraderie but also a focal point of mystery, danger, and potential peril, culminating in a confrontation with a Serpent lurking within the depths of the cave. As the residents and guests navigate the enigmatic occurrences surrounding the farm, its significance in both the local community and the broader realm of discovery continues to deepen.
