Grand Bazaar

The Grand Bazaar is a vibrant marketplace located atop the city, known for its bustling atmosphere and diverse array of shops. It features upscale vendors, particularly Nazif's Scents, a perfumery that attracts a varied clientele. The bazaar's architecture includes a maze of covered walkways, warehouse fronts, and upper-story residences, enhancing its lively ambiance. Colorful awnings stretch overhead, creating a kaleidoscope of colors that draws in both locals and visitors. Merchants sell a variety of goods, including spices, textiles, and perfumes, making the Grand Bazaar a crucial hub for trade and social interaction in the city. The climate is typically warm, allowing it to thrive as a popular gathering place throughout the year. Visitors encounter a mix of inhabitants, including humans and non-humans, reflecting a tapestry of cultures.

Nazif's Scents contains a hidden door that leads to the Undercity. This door opens to well-lit ancient stairs that descend into a stone hall, leading to the Undercity, which is populated by tieflings, half-orcs, and other altered folk. However, in the aftermath of the Festival of Candles, Nazif's perfumery stall was closed following his death at the hands of the Ghost of March. The stall was under a state of emergency when Sharif the Strong Fur, a talking black cat wearing a red fez, claimed he was taking over Nazif's duties. He offered passage to the Undercity for a fee or password and enforced a strict no-magic rule in the stall due to concerns over volatility and secrecy. Sharif also requested assistance for gravely ill cats and implied a connection to the Ghost of March, stating that Nazif had been relieved of his duties by Ghost of March.
