Ennis’ Restaurant

Ennis's Restaurant is a house of dining hard by the Grand Bazaar. It offers a spacious stone room hung with carpets, a comfortable setting for its guests, and is oft given over to occasions of consequence — weddings and the like — with room enough for a various company. The air grows lively at the busy hours, when the space fills with guests, and Ennis, the host, sees to a warm welcome and a table well kept.

The restaurant keeps besides a private back patio, for those who would have a little more ease and quiet.
