Old Perfumery

The Old Perfumery is an active shop known for its bustling atmosphere and several cats that observe from various vantage points. The exterior displays wares for sale that are not immediately recognizable, indicating a unique inventory. Inside, the shop is dimly lit, featuring a dark corner where a white cat named Sharif is often found. Sharif wears a fez and possesses a small sword.

The shop serves as a location where individuals seek information about powerful items. Sharif has tasked patrons with obtaining information from a faceless ghost associated with the races. The shop is frequented by patrons, some of whom do not return, contributing to its mysterious reputation.
