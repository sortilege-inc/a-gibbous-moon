Janissary Safehouse

The Janissary Safehouse is situated in a poor district and is characterized by its secretive nature. The entrance is designed to blend in with its surroundings, making it difficult to detect. This location serves as a concealed meeting point for Janissaries operatives, providing a secure environment for discussions and planning.

A Janissary Lieutenant reported that Alexander, a recent defector, had ventured into a specified area of catacombs a couple of days prior and had not returned. The lieutenant noted that this area was distinct from the "Secret Church" catacombs.
