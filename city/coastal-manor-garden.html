Coastal Manor Garden

The Coastal Manor Garden is a place fallen into ruin, scored with burn-marks and bearing the signs of looting from conflicts past. Once a garden of some beauty, it is now overgrown and mouldering, though remnants of its former charm linger still. It sits upon the coast, and may perhaps be tied to a former ruler, one Alumdar Pasha.

The garden is haunted by shadowy, half-corporeal figures got up in the likeness of Janissaries soldiers, armed and closing upon any who intrude. In such encounters a commanding officer rises amongst the shades, bearing a blade described by Alemdar Mustafa Pasha of March — a sword acknowledged to be of no small significance.
