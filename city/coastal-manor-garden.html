Coastal Manor Garden

The Coastal Manor Garden is a location in disrepair, marked by scorch marks and signs of looting from past conflicts. Once a beautiful garden, it is now overrun and moldering, though remnants of its former charm remain. The area is situated on the coast and is possibly linked to a previous ruler, Alumdar Pasha.

The garden is inhabited by shadowy, semi-corporeal figures resembling Janissaries soldiers, armed and closing in on intruders. During encounters, a commanding officer among the shades appears, wielding a blade described by Alemdar Mustafa Pasha of March, which is recognized as a sword of significance.
