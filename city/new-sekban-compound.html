New Sekban Compound

Ghost HQ is a compound located in the Undercity, featuring an open arched gate guarded by an orcish figure and a halfling in Sekban-adjacent fatigues. The interior includes alternating open-air and enclosed rooms, such as strategy spaces, a training yard, and conference areas. The compound has a modern military design, with strategy rooms displaying maps from historical battles, reflecting a force styled as a modernized “new army” in the Sekban mold. The library is curated with military history of high standards.

A cafeteria within the compound serves communal meals to a diverse group of recruits, many of whom are non-human. The atmosphere is filled with the smell of gunfire and explosive residue, combined with the aromas of fresh bread and stew. Recruits engage in tactical training and strategy discussions throughout the compound. The leader, Alemdar Mustafa Pasha, meets with visitors in a curtained office to discuss matters of unity and strategy, including a request for collaboration with the Janissaries. The compound functions as a hub for military organization and recruitment, emphasizing readiness for potential conflict while maintaining a facade of enmity with other factions.
