Festival of Candles

The Festival of Candles is a vibrant celebration held in an open area of a bustling city, featuring lively activities such as shadow dancers. The event attracts a diverse crowd but is overshadowed by the assassination of Nazif, a perfumist.

During the festival, the Ghost of March and his entourage confront Nazif, accusing him of betrayal and asserting that his responsibilities are being taken over by the Council of the Ninth Life. This confrontation culminates in Nazif's death, witnessed by Chique Manu. Following the assassination, Ghost of March and his followers leave quickly as Bostanji Imperial Guard declare a state of emergency to restore order and disperse the crowd. The atmosphere shifts from celebration to fear and uncertainty, highlighting the ongoing tensions within the city.
