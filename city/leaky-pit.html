Leaky Pit

The Leaky Pit is a vibrant plaza located in the undercity, known as an explosive arts district. It features numerous venues that attract a diverse crowd, including upper-class visitors from the surface. The area hosts various artistic performances and cultural events. Notable establishments include the Styx Bathhouse, the Thieves Bazaar, the Shapeshifter tailor shop, the Ermeydani underground fighting venue, and Misty Pond, a serene subterranean park with clear waters and unique fauna.

Sidra al-Najjar mapped the undercity zones and identified the Leaky Pit as an entertainment hub frequented by a mix of people, including non-human folk, indicating a coexistence of various cultures and backgrounds. The plaza serves as a gateway to the undercity, with a nearby entrance commonly used by those familiar with the area. It is also a gathering place for the community, showcasing a blend of artistic expression and social interaction.
