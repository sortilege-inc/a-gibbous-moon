Spectral Hippodrome

The Hippodrome is an underground venue that hosts magical horse races, attracting a diverse crowd of humans and non-humans, including wealthy patrons and a notable minority of elven spectators. It operates outside the traditional public events associated with the historical Hippodrome plaza, creating a lively and somewhat clandestine atmosphere. The structure, slightly smaller than its historical grandeur, accommodates hundreds of spectators, all eager to place bets on the races, which feature spectral horses that glow Blue and Green. Races are not held on Saturdays, with the next event scheduled for another day, and attendees are advised to "bet on Green."

Gizem Faruki, a local baker, learns about the Hippodrome from neighbors Yusuf and Osman, who mention a new experience there. Osman invites Gizem to the race and warns her that "the horses are not real." The event takes place near the Theodosius Obelisk, which serves as a meeting point. The underground nature of the Hippodrome and the fantastical elements of its races contribute to its allure, making it a focal point for entertainment in the Undercity.

During a recent event, an elven woman was observed performing a necrotic debilitation on one of the spectral horses in the stables, suggesting potential foul play. The races included a Green-team chariot that faltered due to the weakening of one of its horses, leading to crowd frustration. Observers noted the same elven woman whispering to Chique Manu, a bookmaker, indicating possible collusion. After the race, a pervasive enchantment was detected throughout the Hippodrome, indicating that the structure is suffused with magical energy.

More recently, the venue has been referred to as the Spectral Hippodrome, where chariots and their ghostly riders compete in ethereal races. The venue features a stabling area where racers reappear after their runs and is noted for its magical qualities, making it difficult to discern specific magical items amidst the chaos. Interactions at the Hippodrome include a Blue rider who expresses a desire for the death of Emperor Justinian. The races were disrupted when only the Green team appeared on the track, leading to a cancellation and rescheduling of the events. Additionally, it was learned that the Crown of Sargon can be found below the Maiden Tower, a significant location tied to ongoing quests.
