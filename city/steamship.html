Steamship

The Steamship is a bustling non-government vessel, renowned for its lively atmosphere and communal spirit. Central to its design is the Parlor Deck, a vibrant area where passengers gather around oblong tables for poker and dining, while a live band fills the air with music, enhancing the social experience. The deck is adorned with shared sleeping quarters, fostering camaraderie among travelers, though it lacks the privacy of more luxurious accommodations.

The Steamship is not merely a means of transport; it is a stage for unexpected events and encounters. A notable incident unfolded when Osman Hamdi Bey was approached by a woman in distress, leading him and others to investigate a nearby cabin. This seemingly innocuous situation escalated dramatically when the woman revealed herself to be a wicked Shade, igniting a confrontation filled with magic and combat. The atmosphere turned tense as a spell cast by a Singer plunged the deck into darkness, complicating their efforts to manage the supernatural threat. Meanwhile, the ship began to take on water due to a spell rebound, prompting a desperate need for immediate action to avert disaster.

The Steamship, with its blend of leisure and peril, serves as a crucial setting for both social interaction and high-stakes adventure, embodying the unpredictable nature of life at sea.
