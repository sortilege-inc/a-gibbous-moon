Janissary Headquarters

The Janissary Headquarters is a Coastal Manor Garden in disrepair, marked by scorch marks and signs of looting from past conflicts. The area is characterized by shadowy, semi-corporeal Janissaries soldiers who materialize around intruders, armed and closing in. These soldiers exhibit resistance to mundane weapon damage, indicating a spectral nature. The headquarters is associated with a sword described by a commanding officer of the shades, which resembles a sword previously seen on display at this location.
