Misty Pond (2)

The Misty Pond is prized for its natural virtues and serves at once as a place of self-care and of quiet commerce in acquaintance. It offers a large communal pool, and private rooms besides, so that a man may mingle openly or confer unheard as suits his purpose.

It was Cyrus, a grey-bearded dwarf druid and seller of beads, who commended the Misty Pond for its singular qualities, calling it a watering-hole of the neighbourhood. He set it against the Styx Bathhouse, which likewise offers refreshment and a ready traffic in rumour.
