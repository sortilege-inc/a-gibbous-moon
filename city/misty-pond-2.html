Misty Pond (2)

The Misty Pond is known for its natural qualities and serves as a venue for self-care and networking. It features a large communal pool area, with private rooms available for both public mingling and private conversations.

Cyrus, a gray-bearded dwarf druid and bead seller, recommended the Misty Pond for its unique qualities, noting its role as a watering hole in the local community. The bathhouse is contrasted with the Styx Bathhouse, which also serves as a place for refreshment and rumor-sharing.
