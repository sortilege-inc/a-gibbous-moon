Warded Chamber

The Warded Chamber is an underground location distinguished by its historical remnants and runic wards intended to contain malevolent forces. Central to the chamber is a large, broken casket, indicative of a previous binding of something dangerous. The chamber is inhabited by undead entities, including an Undead Priest who consumed the corpses of fellow priests, and various guardians awakened during exploration.

Evidence of a past battle is present, notably in the form of animated armor that acknowledged the completion of its task before deactivating. Artifacts recovered from the chamber include a memoir attributed to Mehmed II. The area indicates a complex history of conflict and containment, underscored by the presence of dangerous entities and remnants of magical confrontations.
