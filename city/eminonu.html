Eminönü

Eminönü is a vibrant neighborhood known for its rich cultural tapestry and bustling atmosphere. It features a mix of residential areas and entertainment hubs, including the Leaky Pit, a plaza recognized for its artistic venues and frequented by both upper-class visitors and residents of the undercity. The area is distinguished by beautiful brickwork and a network of interconnected buildings, some controlled by the Ghost of March. Eminönü serves as a gateway to the undercity, with hidden passages and shortcuts known only to those familiar with its secrets.

Matilda Manukian, a half-elf with access to the undercity, revealed a hidden staircase behind a restaurant that provided a shortcut to the Leaky Pit. This area is described as an explosive arts district, filled with music and dance, where humans mingle with individuals of magical heritage. Matilda also provided directions for navigating the undercity and advice on approaching the Ghost of March.
