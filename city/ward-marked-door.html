Ward-Marked Door

The Ward-Marked Door is a formidable steel structure adorned with intricate runes matching those on a nearby casket. It is located within a Ruin, likely intended as a protective barrier against threats from within. The door lacks a conventional handle or lock, indicating it is opened through magical means. When activated, it lifts into the ceiling with a shuttering motion and emits a booming voice in thick Turkish, suggesting the presence of a guardian or sentinel associated with the door's magic.

Upon opening the Ward-Marked Door, a chamber was revealed, summoning four undead guardians: a Varangian Guard wielding a greataxe, a Janissary armed with a tüfek and a yatagan, a Mounted Warrior with a war pick and kite shield, and a Persian-style grenadier equipped with a spear and grenade. The guardians were ultimately defeated through a combination of spells and melee attacks, leading to the collection of their weapons and armor. The door's magical nature and the ensuing conflict highlight its role as a significant barrier within the Ruin, connecting the guardians to the ancient protective measures still in place.
