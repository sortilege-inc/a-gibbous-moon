Papa Mert’s

Papa Mert’s is an apothecary located in the undercity, serving as a mentor and resource for those seeking knowledge of the medicinal and toxic properties of various substances. The establishment focuses on the safe collection and use of flora, particularly fungi and mosses. It features a workspace where patrons can learn about the properties of different plants, including their medicinal benefits and potential dangers.

Sidra al-Najjar collected samples of moss and fungi from Misty Pond and brought them to Papa Mert’s for assessment. Upon arrival, she was instructed to scrub her hands with “Biyin,” a white powder used to absorb toxins. Papa Mert distinguished between the various samples, explaining their properties and teaching Sidra safer collection methods.

More recently, the area surrounding Papa Mert’s became a locale where a party informed a friendly cat of the Maiden Tower lead for the Crown of Sargon and pledged to pursue it.
