Cemetery

The Cemetery is situated in a middle-class district of the city, characterized by a mix of untended scrub plants and uneven terrain. It features various grave markers, some visible grave stones, and maintains an overall unkempt appearance, which contrasts with many Muslim cemeteries that often lack headstones. The atmosphere is dark and quiet, especially at night, with a palpable stillness occasionally interrupted by faint chittering sounds from hidden creatures.

Small, mischievous creatures known as Scavengers inhabit the Cemetery. They are intrigued by offerings of baklava and candies, and they revealed their plight regarding a witch who has taken their tree, located two hills away. The Scavengers requested cups to drink from in exchange for their treats. The Cemetery served as a significant place for these creatures, linking their mischief to the broader narrative involving Invisible Attacker and the restoration of their home.
