Upper City

The Upper City features a mix of bustling markets and quiet, cluttered alleyways, leading to a variety of warehouse fronts and upper-story residences. The streets are often less active, with some areas becoming increasingly empty, contrasting with the more vibrant life in the Undercity. The climate suggests a typical urban atmosphere, possibly warm and humid, characteristic of market areas.

Inhabitants include a diverse range of people, reflective of a multicultural society. The area is known for its warehouses and upper-story residences, indicating a space where commerce and residence intermingle. Supernatural beings, such as the albasti, have been encountered in the alleyways, suggesting the Upper City is a backdrop for both mundane and extraordinary events. Reports of strange occurrences in the local graveyard hint at a connection between the Upper City and mysterious activities affecting its inhabitants.
