Ermeydani

Ermeydani is an underground no-weapons fight venue known for its heavy betting culture and vibrant sports atmosphere. It operates like a mixed martial arts arena, attracting a diverse crowd willing to engage in combat without armor or weapons. The venue is characterized by enthusiastic patrons drawn to the thrill of the fights and the camaraderie among spectators.

Sidra al-Najjar recently mapped the undercity and identified Ermeydani as a notable location within the entertainment hub surrounding the Leaky Pit. This area is recognized as an explosive arts district, frequented by upper-class visitors who come to enjoy various performances and events. Ermeydani stands out as a place where anyone can participate or spectate, contributing to the lively and inclusive atmosphere of the undercity.
