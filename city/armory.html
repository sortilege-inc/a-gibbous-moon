Armory

The Armory is a secure chamber filled with various weapons and equipment, located within a larger Ruin. It contains eight spears, eight short swords, two quivers of arrows, and two quivers of crossbow bolts, including five +1 bolts. Among the contents are two magical short swords, one of which deals necrotic damage. The room appears to have been well-maintained, suggesting it served as a storeroom for arms and armor.

The Armory was accessed after encounters with undead creatures and a Vampire. Upon entering, the array of weapons was discovered, allowing for the selection of both magical and mundane gear to aid in ongoing efforts against threats within the ruins.
