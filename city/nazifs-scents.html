Nazif’s Scents

Nazif's Scents is a perfumery in the Grand Bazaar, known for an opulent air and rich aromas. A shopkeeper at the front presses upon the visitor a whole range of perfumes, from the cheap to the costly, in a manner that betrays a settled culture of the upsell. Its master, Nazif, is a tall man in a loose shirt who has built a prosperous trade and draws a clientele of every sort.

Nazif's Scents serves besides as a gateway to the Undercity. Behind a thick wooden door lie well-lit stone stairs, and at their foot a spacious stone hall hung with carpets, which opens in its turn upon the bustling Undercity and its people — tieflings, half-orcs, and half-elves.
