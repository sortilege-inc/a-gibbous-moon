Nazif’s Scents

Nazif's Scents is a perfumery located in the Grand Bazaar, known for its opulent atmosphere and rich aromas. The shopfront has a shopkeeper who promotes a range of perfumes, from budget options to upscale fragrances, indicating a culture of upselling. The owner, Nazif, is a tall man in a loose shirt who has established a prosperous business that attracts a diverse clientele.

Nazif's Scents serves as a gateway to the Undercity. It features a thick wooden door that leads to well-lit stone stairs. Descending these stairs opens into a spacious stone hall adorned with carpets, which leads into a bustling Undercity inhabited by tieflings, half-orcs, and half-elves.
