Misty Pond

Misty Pond is a subterranean park featuring clear waters and unusual fauna. The area is lush with mosses and fungi, creating an oasis-like atmosphere. It serves as a tranquil retreat within the undercity, where visitors can observe various creatures that frequent the watering hole. Paths wind around the pond, leading to other parts of the undercity and potentially connecting to the upper city.

Sidra al-Najjar mapped various zones of the undercity, noting Misty Pond as a significant feature. She collected samples of moss and fungi from the area for further study. The park attracts both locals and visitors from the upper city, serving as a gathering place within the underground community.
