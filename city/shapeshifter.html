Shapeshifter

The Shapeshifter is an elite shop of disguise and tailoring in the undercity, known for a craft equal to any need, the making of disguises among them. It is kept by a dwarf proprietor and holds a wide array of fabrics and styles, so that a customer may treat for whatever custom outfit he pleases. Its atmosphere answers to the creativity of the arts district about it, its wares stacked high and its energy never still.

The shop stands as a fair example of that marriage of artistry and practicality one finds throughout the undercity, and is a place of some consequence to any who would alter his appearance, to whatever end.
