The Pearl

The Pearl is an upscale brothel and massage parlor managed by Mistress Manukian, located in a lively area of the city. It features a club-like atmosphere with music and a combination of public spaces and semi-private booths separated by curtains, allowing for discreet conversations. The establishment caters to a higher-end clientele and offers both sexual and massage services.

Inside, Jean-Zera Mongé met with an Arab man representing an assassin to discuss a ruse involving a plot against Mustafa Pasha, the vizier, aimed at drawing out responses while keeping the actual target undisclosed. After their conversation, the party used a Memory-Wipe Scroll to erase the representative's recollection of the meeting.
