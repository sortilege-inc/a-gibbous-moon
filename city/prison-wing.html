Prison Wing

The Prison Wing is a subterranean complex with dark, oppressive atmosphere and dilapidated stone walls. It features a series of cells that echo with the sounds of restless prisoners, some restrained and others roaming freely. The area is dimly lit, with remnants of ancient fixtures suggesting a once functional lighting system. A prominent feature of the wing is a hole in the ceiling, which serves as a potential escape route but is blocked by rubble. The complex has undergone significant decay, with broken chains and shattered vials scattered across the ground. An aura of dark magic lingers, indicative of its history of containing supernatural threats.

The wing includes a Laboratory filled with broken vials and petrified herbs and an Armory stocked with various weapons, such as magical short swords and crossbows. Undead guardians pose a significant threat within the wing. The area contains valuable artifacts and resources, including holy water and weapons.
