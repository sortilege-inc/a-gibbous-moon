Bound Casket

The Bound Casket is an empty casket with broken chains, located in a room that formerly housed significant wards, as indicated by runic symbols. It is believed to have contained remains that pose a danger, suggesting its purpose was the containment of something perilous. The ghost of Radu the Handsome indicates that the casket relates to the remains of his brother, who was previously bound and not entirely inert.

After the destruction of a vampire, the party inspects the casket, confirming its emptiness and the absence of attached chains. A jinn connected to Sidra al-Najjar expresses relief at the lack of an existential threat, suggesting that the casket's original purpose was to prevent a greater evil. The presence of the casket and its broken chains implies it served as a safeguard against the vampire's return or resurrection, highlighting themes of containment and release.
