Knock Spell Scroll

The Knock spell scroll is a magical item available for purchase in the undercity, priced at 275 gold pieces. It allows the user to cast the Knock spell, which unlocks doors and containers that are magically sealed. This spell is particularly useful for gaining access to restricted areas or hidden treasures.

Sidra al-Najjar discovered the Knock spell scroll while browsing the Thieves' Bazaar. She noted its existence but indicated she did not have sufficient funds to purchase it. The scroll's availability was mentioned alongside other items, highlighting its potential utility for adventurers.
