Crown of Sargon

The Crown of Sargon is a powerful item associated with a faceless ghost who stated its location is "below the Maiden Tower." The ghost mentioned that certain cats seek the item, indicating its significance in their dealings. The Crown is linked to the historical figure Sargon, suggesting a connection to ancient Mesopotamian royalty.

During a conversation at the Spectral Hippodrome, a Blue rider confirmed the Crown's location to Sidra al-Najjar. He expressed a desire for the death of Emperor Justinian, stating that knowledge of the Crown's whereabouts was no longer necessary due to Justinian's death. The rider's sudden departure from the races after this revelation suggests the Crown's potential to influence events surrounding the ghost and the quest.
