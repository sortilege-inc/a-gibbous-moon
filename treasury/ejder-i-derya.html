Ejder-i Derya

Ejder-i Derya is a steamboat that recently experienced a crisis, taking on water and necessitating emergency repairs. During this incident, a djinn was banished, and a woman serving as an exorcism decoy escaped via teleportation with accomplices. The ship's hull was breached but has since appeared to be magically remade. The captain announced that the emergency had stabilized, and Ejder-i Derya would head directly to Constantinople.

After reaching Constantinople, significant events unfolded, including the arrival of Crown Prince Abd-ul Mejid, who informed the crew of the Caliph's death and enlisted the aid of Osman Hamdi Bey. Ejder-i Derya served as a setting for subsequent interactions and tasks, including a mission to recover a stolen journal that had obscured Osman Hamdi Bey's memories. The ship's journey involved discussions, planning, and preparations for infiltration into the criminal underbelly of the city, starting with Hano "the Widow."
