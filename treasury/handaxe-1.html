Handaxe +1

The handaxe +1 is a magical weapon given to Gizem by Hanno, a monster-hunting patron, in recognition of the group's successful encounter with the albasti. It is an enhanced version of a standard handaxe, with specific details about its appearance and additional magical properties not provided.

After the confrontation with the albasti, the group transported the creature's inert body to Hanno's establishment. Hanno approved of their trophy and rewarded them with 25 gold pieces to be shared among the group, in addition to the handaxe +1 for Gizem. This transaction highlights the value of tangible rewards for successful monster hunting within the community.
