Nazar Bead

The Nazar bead is a magical object that acts as a protective talisman against malevolent forces. It is notably referenced when Sidra al-Najjar crushed the bead and blew the dust at the albasti, a dream-feeding creature, thereby cursing it to impair the creature's next attack or ability check with ill fortune.

The bead is commonly associated with the concept of the "evil eye," a symbol believed to ward off harm and ill will. In an encounter with the albasti, Sidra employed the Nazar bead strategically, indicating its significance against supernatural threats. The act of crushing the bead and invoking its power highlights its utility in combat and protection against malevolent entities.
