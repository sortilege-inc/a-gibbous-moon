scroll of Greater Restoration

The scroll of Greater Restoration is a magical scroll recovered from a waterproof metal box in the cistern. Jean-Zera identified it as a scroll of Greater Restoration and read Petrus Gyllius’s diary found alongside it.

An arcana test revealed that the scroll contains a person trapped inside, who could potentially be freed using Greater Restoration or Remove Curse.
