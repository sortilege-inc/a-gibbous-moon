Necklace Of Prayer Beads

The Necklace of Prayer Beads is an enchanted string reckoned at five hundred gold, its beads stirring with movement and colour, and put to ritual and magical uses. It is sold by Cyrus, a grey-bearded dwarf druid who keeps a public-facing trade in beads and stands as something of a pillar of his community.

Gizem Faruki looked over Cyrus's wares, the Necklace of Prayer Beads amongst them, whilst seeking her bearings in the undercity; and her host obliged her with counsel upon where one might go for equipment and for refreshment.
