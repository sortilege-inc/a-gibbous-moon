Non-Government Steamer

The Non-Government Steamer is a maritime vessel featuring cargo, sleeping, and parlor decks, facilitating transport for various individuals. It serves as a mode of travel for characters such as Osman Hamdi Bey, who occupies a private room, while others share berths. The designation as a non-government vessel suggests a more informal or potentially illicit nature.

During the journey, the steamer hosts activities such as chess games and social interactions among its passengers. The atmosphere is lively, enhanced by a live band and opportunities for gambling. The captain of the steamer is a Greek veteran smuggler, indicating possible connections to clandestine activities. A crisis occurs when a spell rebound from a ritual circle causes a breach in the hull, resulting in the ship taking on water. This incident leads characters to alert the captain and seek assistance, highlighting the vessel's precarious situation as it begins to sink.
