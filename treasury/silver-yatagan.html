Silver Yatagan

The Silver Yatagan is a curved Ottoman sword awarded to Sidra al-Najjar by Hannah for resolving the issue of an evil spirit in the cemetery. The sword features a distinctive curvature characteristic of yatagans, known for their effective slashing abilities.

In addition to the yatagan, Hannah provided a set of 30 silver-tipped throwing knives and a monetary reward of 200 gold pieces. Following the award, Sidra al-Najjar and Ahmet Yusuf discussed the division of the rewards. Sidra retained the Silver Yatagan, while Ahmet took 150 gold pieces, leaving Sidra with 50 gold. The throwing knives were evenly divided, with each receiving 15 knives.
