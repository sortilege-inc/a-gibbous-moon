Memory-Wipe Scroll

The Memory-Wipe Scroll is an enchanted paper that strikes particular memories from a mind. It holds two charges of that memory-wiping working, which makes it a serviceable thing where discretion and secrecy are wanted. It was used upon a representative of an assassin to erase his recollection of a conversation, and so to keep a mission whole.
