Dispelling Beads

Dispelling Beads are magical items available for purchase from Cyrus, a gray-bearded dwarf druid known for selling ritual and magical items. These beads have the ability to negate or dispel magical effects. Cyrus also offers beads of force and a necklace of prayer beads. The beads are noted for their beauty, with some displaying movement and color.

Gizem Faruki visited Cyrus's shop and browsed the various magical items, including the Dispelling Beads. During this visit, Cyrus provided guidance on other locations in the undercity, including Sif’s Books and Manuscripts as a notable place to visit.
