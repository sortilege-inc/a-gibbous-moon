Malkoç Family Banner

The Malkoç Family Banner is an artifact of the old Coach family, encased in glass within a private library. It was discovered alongside a Turkish treatise by Radu the Handsome detailing military tactics and a karabela saber dedicated to John III Sobieski, King of Poland.

The banner was retrieved following encounters with undead and a vampire, showcasing the party's combat and magical skills. After defeating the vampire, they searched various rooms and located the Malkoç Family Banner among other artifacts. Correspondence found with the banner linked it to significant figures such as Mehmed II and Vlad III, though specific details regarding its design or symbolism are not documented.
