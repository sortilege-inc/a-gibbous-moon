Memoir Attributed To Mehmed II

The memoir attributed to Mehmed II is a metal-framed text found in the satchel of Hyria, a leader of East African tomb raiders. It is several hundred years old and archaeologically valuable. The memoir contains correspondence related to the rule of Wallachia and the Ottoman court, including discussions involving Vlad III and Radu the Handsome. Its significance lies in its connection to historical figures and events, particularly regarding the power dynamics of the time.
