Permanent-Entry Coins

Permanent-entry coins, known as "fast passes," are a form of currency that grants ongoing access to specific locations, particularly the undercity. A group acquired these coins after negotiating a purchase, overpaying for the privilege of easy entry. This transaction eliminated the need to pay standard fees for each visit, facilitating their exploration of the undercity. The acquisition of these coins significantly enhanced their ability to navigate the undercity more freely and effectively.
