Wondrous Item · Artefact

burlap bag

The burlap bag is a hanging sack made of burlap-like material that has not rotted and is magical. It possesses an abjuration effect that resists aging and deterioration, preserving its contents. The bag was found suspended from a platform edge. Jean-Zera detected its magic, used Mage Hand to tug it, and then hauled it up by its rope. When opened, the bag contained an old but pristine hand drum. The party kept both the bag and the drum.
