Wondrous Item · Artefact

wide triangular arrowhead

The wide triangular arrowhead is an artifact discovered within a serpent's nest during a cave exploration. It is designed to be affixed to a standard crossbow bolt or arrow shaft, enabling it to be fired as part of a normal attack. This arrowhead delivers an additional 1d10 poison damage upon impact and can be retrieved and reused after being fired.

Rufus Dimitrious initially found the arrowhead while descending into the serpent's hole, among other items such as eggs and valuables. He chose to leave the arrowhead behind along with a bronze goblet, taking only a necklace and several ancient coins. Later, the arrowhead was identified by Rufus and Jean-Zera Mongé, who confirmed its suitability for mounting and poison delivery. The poisoned arrowhead was ultimately retained by the party as part of their collected relics.
