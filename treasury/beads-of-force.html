Beads Of Force

The Beads of Force are enchanted trinkets offered for sale by Cyrus, a grey-bearded dwarf druid, and stand amongst a wider collection of ritual and magical wares — dispelling beads, a necklace of prayer beads, and the like. They are things of no little beauty, some of them shifting in colour as one turns them in the light.

Cyrus's shop is known for its decorative and ritual beadwork. Gizem Faruki called there, hoping to acquire what might aid her and her companions, and her host obliged her with something of the temper of the undercity and a word or two upon the reputation his shop enjoys for such magical goods.
