Sword of Yakub Agha

The Sword of Yakub Agha is a weapon associated with an assassination attempt on the Ghost of March. The Ghost provided a detailed description of the sword, which resembled one displayed at the Janissary headquarters. The Ghost requested its retrieval, believing it would aid in making peace with ARA.

The sword was located in a coastal manor garden in disrepair, showing signs of past conflicts, including scorch marks and looting. During the investigation, shadowy, semi-corporeal janissary soldiers materialized, armed and closing in.
