The Folk of the City

Mahidevran Hatun

Mahidevran Hatun is a young woman dressed in a silk green dress. She resides in a richly furnished cell that contains rugs, chairs, tables, and a glass of drink. When the party peered through a viewing window, she inquired if Suleiman was ready for her, identified herself as Suleiman’s favorite concubine, and mentioned that she had been secluded for a few days for her own safety.
