The Folk of the City

Hussein

Hussein is the operator of the Styx Bathhouse in the Undercity. He has a hirsute appearance, characterized by thick black ringlets of hair. Hussein engages with patrons in a professional manner, discussing the operations of the bathhouse as a venue for gathering rumors and conducting business deals. He invites visitors to share their experiences and knowledge about bathhouses above, showing curiosity about their operations.

Hussein explains that the Styx Bathhouse serves as a place for people to mingle and trade information, similar to a tea house. Public discussions occur in the main area, while private conversations take place in secluded rooms, accommodating both casual interactions and serious business dealings based on the participants' needs.
