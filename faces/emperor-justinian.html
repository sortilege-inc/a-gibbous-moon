Emperor Justinian

Justinian

Emperor Justinian is a historical figure associated with significant power and influence during the reign of Queen Theodora. He is connected to The Shahmeran, a powerful and dangerous entity bound beneath the Basilica Cistern since his era. The Blue Rider expresses a desire for Justinian's death, indicating that his legacy continues to impact the present. The Blue Rider is surprised to learn that Justinian has been dead for a long time, suggesting that Justinian's influence or memory still lingers in the world.
