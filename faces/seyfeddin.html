Seyfeddin

Seyfeddin is an old Janissary,who picked up the career of a mercenary after the Hearth was abolished and cast out of the Imperial Military. His professionalism is immense, and he does not protest working against the empire he once served. He is a powerful fighter, well-trained in close combat. He is part of Hamit's crew that attempted to assassinate the vizier-i.
