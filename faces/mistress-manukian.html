The Folk of the City

Mistress Manukian

Mistress Manukian is associated with The Pearl, an upscale brothel and massage parlor. She commands attention and moves with authority and confidence. She gestures across the room to indicate where a party is waiting, reflecting her role in managing the establishment and facilitating meetings.

She hands over a scroll containing two instances of the Exorcise Genie spell and a bag of 200 gold to Sidra al-Najjar, indicating her involvement in transactions related to bounties and magical resources.
