Evelyn Calvert

Evelyn Calvert is a member of the Calvert family, residing at Thymbra Farm, north of Constantinople. She is married to Frederick Calvert, and they host prominent guests such as Osman Hamdi Bey and General Patrice de MacMahon.

Evelyn is skilled at navigating discussions on topics like war and trade, showcasing her influence in social settings. During a dinner at their farmhouse, she engages with guests, adeptly managing the dynamics of the table and facilitating conversation. Evelyn contrasts with Sophia, a younger guest, who is less confident in social interactions. Her presence is smooth and assured, reflecting her familiarity with the social environment of the gathering.
