The Folk of the City

Deniz

Deniz is a druid who is known only in goat form and stewards an oasis in the Undercity. Her humanoid form has not been encountered, and her specific appearance remains unknown.

Miray planned to seek out Deniz among other druids, indicating her recognition within the community. The oasis she tends suggests a connection to nature and a role in providing resources or support to those in the Undercity.
