The Folk of the City

Deniz

Deniz is a druid known only in the shape of a goat, who tends an oasis within the Undercity. Her human form none has met, and what she looks like remains a matter of guesswork.

Miray meant to seek Deniz out among the other druids, which speaks to the regard she is held in by that community; and the oasis she keeps argues a bond with nature, and some office in the giving of aid and sustenance to those who dwell in the Undercity.
