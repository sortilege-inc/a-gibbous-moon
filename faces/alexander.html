Alexander

Alexander is a former colleague of Alemdar Mustafa Pasha who has left the Ghost's forces to work under Ahra. He has a similar pedigree to Jean-Zera, who has an elephant parent. Alemdar expresses a desire to understand the reasons behind Alexander's departure, suggesting it may have been due to some form of disgruntlement or disagreement. Alemdar recommends that Jean-Zera seek out Alexander to gain insight into his motivations for leaving.

Recently, Alexander, a male character in his early 30s, defected to the Janissaries. He was involved in a conflict that resulted in the death of his wife, Nadia, who was killed for using magic. A couple of days prior to the current events, he ventured into a specific area of catacombs and has not returned since. His whereabouts are currently unknown, and he is sought after for his potential as a mentor to Shehrazat, a young girl with unstable magic.

During an encounter in the catacombs, Alexander warned the party to leave, insisting they had seen nothing. He attempted to maintain control of his repeating handgun when Gizem Faruki rushed to disarm him. After being subdued by the party, he agreed to mentor a young person with uncontrollable magic, provided that his vendetta against Opera was not disclosed and his location remained hidden. Alexander indicated that there had been four defectors in total, some of whom had met grim ends in the catacombs. He discussed the need to prevent further undead manifestations from the bodies in the tunnels. He assisted the party in their efforts against a Giant Skeleton, attempting to shoot it but suffering a misfire before successfully landing hits from the flank. Following the battle, he was escorted to a safe location in the undercity, where he received temporary lodging provided by Papa Mert, aided by a disguise kit and shared cloak.

More recently, the party checked on Alexander with Scheherazad, discovering that he was protected behind a strong concealment ward. He is expected to help the Ghost of March, with contact arranged via a calico alley cat.
