Vizier-i Aezam

Mustafa Pasha is Vizier-i Aezam. He is the target of a plot involving a ruse to create a distraction regarding his life at Topkapi Palace. The plan aims to elicit responses from those who protect him. The action against Mustafa Pasha is set to occur before a meeting with the Kavalan Of Egypt, scheduled for two days later.

Mustafa Pasha the Vizier-i Azam is a prominent figure in the Ottoman Empire, targeted for assassination by Hamit the Quartz of Damascus to disrupt peace talks with Egypt. In response, the party devised a plan to protect him by using him as bait at Yedikule Fortress. They executed a decoy operation, bringing the real Vizier in quietly, disguised as an ordinary prisoner, and placing him in a Yedikule Fortress. A decoy was escorted with moderate fanfare to the Yedikule Fortress. The true identity of the prisoner was withheld from the Mansur Enforcers to mitigate the risk of moles. The party's efforts culminated in the capture of Hamit, who was later interrogated after his memory of the encounter was erased using Bergüzar's memory-erasing scroll.
