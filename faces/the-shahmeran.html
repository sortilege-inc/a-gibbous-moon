The Shahmeran

Shahmeran

The Shahmeran is bound beneath the Basilica Cistern since the time of Justinian and Theodora. The last fight against her and her spellcasters occurred during that era. Alemdar Mustafa Pasha of March reacted sharply when the name "The Shahmeran" was mentioned, showing a significant level of concern regarding this entity.

More recently, she has been observed in a different form as a The Shahmeran named Shamroan, who wears a form-fitting red dress and a pearl pendant. She appears to be in her 30s. When encountered, she asked whether the party had come "to fight or to free us," and identified herself with "Shamroan will suffice," stating that her powers were suppressed. She mentioned that Osman had visited her years earlier and inquired if the party sought aid in avenging his killers. After the party passed Osman’s journal materials through the bars, she read them, declared that Osman had tasked the party, and called herself their ally. When the party pried the damaged cell door off and freed her, the anti-magic field dissipated, and she broke into manic laughter, revealing herself to be a sorceress who had been going along with the party’s assumption that she was the Shamarin.
