The Folk of the City

Shehrazat

Scheherazad

Shehrazat is a young tiefling with ram horns and blue-purple skin, marked by hardy, cracked skin on her forearms, which indicates a severe condition. Beneath layers of clothing and a thin blanket, Shehrazat shivers, reflecting her fragile state.

When Sidra entered her modest living space, Shehrazat was found on a cushion bed, appearing feverish and unable to move effectively. She expressed gratitude when offered food and warmth. Shehrazat mentioned that she had been ill for just under two weeks, suffering from a condition referred to as "blood rot," which began after an involuntary surge of her innate magic. She stated that her Grandmama delivered meals and laundry but avoided close contact due to fear of her illness rebounding. Aware of her situation, she acknowledged she had been advised to seek Dr. Feuerbach at the Pera Palace Hotel, but was unable to go above ground.

After receiving healing spells from Jean-Zera and Ahmet, Shehrazat experienced a rebound effect that caused her to convulse briefly before settling into deep, steady breathing, ultimately resting with a healing paste applied to her affected areas.

Recently, she has expressed a deep concern about her unstable magic, revealing a mental closet filled with dangerous magic that she knows she should not open. Shehrazat desires to find Alexander as a mentor who can assist her in managing her magical issues. In a visit from Sidra al-Najjar, she invited her in to help her eat and shared her thoughts, stating that while she can maintain her environment to avoid accessing the dangerous magic, she would feel more secure if she knew how to manage it properly.

More recently, Shehrazat was checked on by the party along with Alexander. She remains safe under a strong concealment ward, and future contact has been arranged via a calico alley cat.
