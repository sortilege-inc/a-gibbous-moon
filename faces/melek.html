Melek

The Tiefling Caster is a humanoid with infernal features, including pointed horns and a tail. They possess spellcasting abilities, demonstrated through the use of various spells during encounters. The tiefling is affiliated with a group of adventurers that includes Sidra al-Najjar, Jean-Zera Mongé, and Gizem Faruki.

During an encounter in the Undercity, the Tiefling Caster initially casts an unidentified spell with no immediate visible effect, suggesting strategic intent. They later cast Spare the Dying to stabilize Jean-Zera Mongé after he is knocked unconscious, and follow this by using Cure Wounds to help Jean-Zera regain consciousness. Their actions demonstrate a willingness to assist their companions, and they engage in dialogue that reflects their role in the group dynamic. The Tiefling Caster's involvement in the skirmish reveals their commitment to the party and highlights their spellcasting prowess, crucial for the group's survival during confrontations.
