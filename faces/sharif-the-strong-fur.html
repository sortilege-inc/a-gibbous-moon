The Folk of the City

Sharif the Strong Fur

Sharif the Strong Fur the Strong Fur is a black cat known for wearing a small red fez and serving as the gatekeeper of the Undercity. He has taken over the responsibilities previously held by Nazif, a perfumist, and enforces a strict no-magic policy at his location, citing concerns about volatility and secrecy. Sharif the Strong Fur claims authority over all cats, stating that they report to him following a legendary overthrow of monkeys in Istanbul. He operates based on mutual understanding rather than following orders from the Ghost of March, indicating his independent standing.

In a covert entry into Nazif’s Perfumery, Sharif the Strong Fur revealed himself to a group, addressing them from beneath a table. He requested coin or a password for passage into the Undercity. When a member of the group attempted to cast a spell, Sharif the Strong Fur slashed their ankle to reinforce his anti-magic rule. He engaged with Sidra al-Najjar, asking for her assistance with four gravely ill cats and hinting at a larger issue requiring coordination.

Sharif the Strong Fur the Strong Fur's influence extends to guarding a specific route to the University, with the cats under his guidance holding a shared stance on magic. He is an enigmatic figure associated with the Council of the Ninth Life, known for maintaining order within the Undercity. His role is linked to the broader governance of the Undercity, which is known for its self-governance and the presence of the "ghost," an individual leader who maintains order.

Recently, during a chaotic encounter in the Undercity involving various factions, including a group led by a dwarf known as Mr. Karen, Sharif the Strong Fur was referenced as a figure with whom the characters might seek an audience. Alemdar Mustafa Pasha, the reputed dead Sekban commander, noted that Sharif the Strong Fur reported favorably on the deeds of the party, highlighting his significant role within the dynamics of the Undercity. The connection to the Council of the Ninth Life indicates a network focused on overseeing and protecting the interests of those living in the Undercity.
