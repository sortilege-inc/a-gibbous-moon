The Folk of the City

Kasap Yusuf

Orc

The Orc Brute is a large and bulky figure, easily recognizable in a crowd due to his significant size. He wields a heavy axe and exhibits a reckless fighting style, often telegraphing his attacks, which reduces his effectiveness in combat. His imposing physical presence suggests raw power but reflects a lack of finesse in battle.

During a confrontation in the Undercity, the Orc Brute charges at Gizem Faruki but misses with wild swings. He engages in a series of reckless attacks, knocking Jean-Zera Mongé unconscious with a heavy blow. Despite his strength, he faces considerable resistance; he fails to resist a sleep spell cast by Sidra al-Najjar and sustains severe damage from Gizem Faruki's flurry of strikes. At one point, he demonstrates his physical might by grabbing and throwing Gizem aside. He attempts to negotiate with the party, demanding "10 para" as payment for their passage. Ultimately, the orc is subdued as the party overcomes his attacks and incapacitates him amidst the skirmish.
