The Folk of the City

Ziya

Ziya is a shadow operative known for her stealth and sharp eyes, instrumental in consolidating power for Kavalan. She is recognized for her attention to small details. During the assault on Yedikule Fortress, Ziya provided ranged fire support while her companion Hamza engaged in melee combat. She participated in the attack alongside Hamit and others, pushing toward the Royal Cell area to reach the decoy Vizier.

As the crew approached the fortress, Ziya covered Hamza with bow and gunfire during the initial melee at the external barracks. She was involved in the fighting that erupted as they entered the prison level, where they encountered hidden veteran fighters posing as prisoners. Throughout the assault, Ziya and her crew sustained injuries and consumed multiple potions to maintain their combat effectiveness.
