Hamit the Quartz of Damascus

Hamit the Quartz of Damascus is the top assassin of Kavalan of Egypt. He is a potion master and expert gunslinger, equipped with pistols, revolvers, hand crossbows, and tüfeqs. Hamit wields a magic scimitar known as Thorn of the Dune, which is adorned with thorns and grants a +2 bonus. He has a high dexterity, reflected in his AC of 17 due to Mage Armor, and possesses 90 HP. His skills include a +13 bonus in Acrobatics, Sleight of Hand, and Stealth. He has access to various potions, including Invisibility, Speed, Flying, Fire Breath, Hill Giant Strength, Climbing, Healing, and Poison Resistance.

Hamit planned a midnight assassination of Vizier-i Aezam to disrupt the Ottoman–Egyptian peace talks. During the assault on Yedikule Fortress, he and his crew approached at night, confirming the exterior was quiet before entering through the main gate. Hamit engaged in melee combat alongside his crew, which included Ziya and Hamza. He pushed toward the Royal Cell area to reach the decoy Vizier. However, he was ultimately captured. After his capture, his memory of encountering Sublime Porte was erased using Bergüzar's memory scroll.

More recently, an unnamed operative sent by Kavalan Pasha was tasked with identifying Hamit's tactical position. The Sublime Porte wanted the assassin caught in the act, and the operative, characterized by a mustache, met Jean-Zera Mongé inside The Pearl to outline a decoy plot that involved leaking a threat on Mustafa Pasha at Topkapi Palace, with action planned before Kavalan of Egypt's meeting in two days. Afterwards, the party used a Memory-Wipe Scroll to erase Hamit's recollection of the conversation. During a diplomatic meeting, he was killed by Kavalan, who suspected that Hamit's memories had been wiped and sought to question him through death.
