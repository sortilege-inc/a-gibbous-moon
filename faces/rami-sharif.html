Rami Sharif

Rami Sharif is an Egyptian business leader recently arrived from Paris. Recognized in the gambling club, he embodies professionalism. Rami is a male in his 40s or older, participating in high-stakes card games. He offers a stake of 200 gold to Gizem Faruki to join a more competitive game, with the condition that the amount will be repaid only from winnings.

During the card game, Rami remains winless, and tension rises at the table, particularly with another player named Rashad. His demeanor is serious and businesslike; he engages in cordial small talk while primarily focusing on the stakes. Rami interacts with other players, including Chique Manu and Rashad, reflecting his deep involvement in the gambling scene without appearing dishonest in his dealings. After Gizem Faruki wins the game and repays him, Rami's presence in the club highlights the high-stakes environment of the evening.
