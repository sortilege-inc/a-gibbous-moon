The Folk of the City

Theodora

Theodora is a powerful entity bound beneath the Basilica Cistern since the time of Justinian and Theodora. She is associated with extraordinary power and has influenced events surrounding the Padishah’s Consort’s sister, a powerful diviner.

Alemdar Mustafa Pasha of March reacts sharply when Theodora is mentioned, indicating her notoriety. Alemdar Mustafa Pasha describes her as a final boss in a historical conflict against spellcasters, highlighting her pivotal role in the power dynamics of that era. Theodora's connection to The Shahmeran further emphasizes her significance, as The Shahmeran is described as extraordinarily powerful and dangerous.
