The Folk of the City

Theodora

Theodora is a being of great power, bound beneath the Basilica Cistern since the days of Justinian and Theodora. Extraordinary power is ascribed to her, and her hand is felt in the affairs that surround the sister of the Padishah’s Consort, herself a diviner of no mean strength.

Alemdar Mustafa Pasha of March starts sharply whenever Theodora is named, which is testimony enough to her ill fame. He speaks of her as the last and greatest adversary of a bygone struggle against the spellcasters, and so marks her a pivotal figure in the balances of power of that age. Her tie to The Shahmeran does but underscore her weight, for The Shahmeran herself is reckoned extraordinarily powerful and dangerous.
