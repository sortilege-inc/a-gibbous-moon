The Folk of the City

Frank the Gnome

Frank is a gnome with a chaotic evil alignment, quick and agile, skilled at pickpocketing. During a confrontation in a crowded alley, he attempted to steal from Jean-Zera Mongé, but withdrew when confronted. Frank carries a pistol, which he used to shoot Jean-Zera, grazing him before retreating among crates and tarps. He wears a cloak that enhances his stealth.

In conflict, Frank displayed defiance and aggression, threatening a party that sought to reclaim stolen funds intended for children. He struggled to keep a heavy bag of coin containing 58 gold equivalent but was disarmed by Jean-Zera. When challenged, Frank aimed his pistol at Jean-Zera, demanding to know his course of action. Despite his threats, he was ultimately outmatched as Jean-Zera successfully took the bag from him, illustrating Frank's role as a chaotic adversary in the broader conflict with the party and other muggers.
