The Folk of the City

Cyrus

Cyrus is a gray-bearded dwarf druid and bead craftsman associated with the Thieves Bazaar. He creates both mundane and magical beads made of glass and painted ceramics, operating his business in the Undercity, where he is a resource for those seeking connections and information. His shop offers various beads, including magical ones such as Beads Of Force and Dispelling Beads, and he possesses a notably expensive Necklace Of Prayer Beads.

Cyrus has gray hair braided at the back and wears a large beaded necklace, reminiscent of a gold chain, symbolizing his status as a community pillar. He is known for his influential opinions that may not align with the broader views of druids. He welcomed a visitor, referring to them as "sister," indicating familiarity with fellow druids. His connections may provide insights into the Ghost of March and knowledge of various aliases, making his craftsmanship and ties to the Undercity community valuable for navigating the area's complex dynamics.

When asked for advice on places to visit in the Undercity, he recommended the Styx Bathhouse for self-care and networking, while refusing to engage in gossip.
