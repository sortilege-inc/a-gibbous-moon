Radu the Handsome

Radu

Radu the Handsome is a ghostly figure with an ethereal presence, manifesting as a spectral entity likely linked to his past life. He expresses gratitude to the party for freeing and subsequently destroying his brother, a Vampire, indicating that this has lifted his burden and allowed him to depart from the location where he was bound.

Radu addresses the party, thanking them for their efforts and gestures towards a Bound Casket in the room, suggesting that the remains within should be dealt with. He reassures the party that there are no lingering threats from his brother's phylactery or coffin. His final words convey a sense of relief before he vanishes, marking a peaceful departure from the mortal realm. Radu is also associated with a Turkish treatise on military tactics, discovered by the party in a Private Library, highlighting his historical significance.

Radu is connected to a historical lineage associated with the Church and his father, Vlad. He is linked to significant artifacts of historical value that are pre-Janissaries and sought after by collectors, indicating his background is intertwined with important historical events. His name arises in discussions about the implications of his family's past and its relevance to current political tensions. Various factions, including the Ghost of March, are considered in relation to Radu's lineage and its potential impact on dealings with these groups. His connection to Osman is also noted as the risks associated with their relationships are assessed, highlighting the influence of Radu's family history on their actions.

Radu's presence is significant in the group's strategic discussions, particularly regarding the need to navigate the complexities of their environment while considering his family's legacy and the implications of their actions in the undercity and beyond. Recently, his insights have become even more crucial as the party faces escalating tensions, and they find themselves relying on his historical knowledge to guide their decisions in a rapidly changing political landscape.
