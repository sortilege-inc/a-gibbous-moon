The Folk of the City

Nadia

Nadia was a human woman known for her use of magic, which ultimately led to her death at the hands of Ahra 'the Wolf' and her associates. She experienced episodes that resulted in multiple house fires and other issues, reflecting her struggle to control her magical abilities.

Her husband, Alexander, expressed deep grief over her loss, stating that she had been killed in cold blood for her use of magic. Following her death, he sought revenge but also recognized the potential to help others, particularly a young person with uncontrollable magic, in her memory. This connection to Nadia influenced his decision to mentor someone else, shifting his focus from vengeance to guidance.
