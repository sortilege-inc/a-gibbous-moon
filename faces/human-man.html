The Folk of the City

human man

The human skeleton was found in an unlocked cell alongside an elven woman. In the cell, a spellbook, a note, and two healing potions were recovered. The spellbook contained Disguise Self, Shield, Magic Missile, and Silent Image. The note and surrounding evidence identified the pair as lovers from Justinian’s time and stated that the human had been working on a spell to save the elf from an unnatural physiology.
