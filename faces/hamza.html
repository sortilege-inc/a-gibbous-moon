Hamza

Hamza is a barbarian with a Strength score of 20 and high hit points. He has resistance to piercing and slashing damage, including from magical sources. Hamza wields a great axe and utilizes combat abilities such as Reckless Attack and Frenzy, making him effective in brutal melee combat.

During the assault on Yedikule Fortress, Hamza kicked down the external barracks door and engaged in fierce melee combat while his companions provided ranged support. He absorbed significant punishment during the fight, sustaining mounting damage and consuming multiple potions to recover. His actions contributed to the crew's efforts to clear the ground floor of the fortress, establishing him as a frontline fighter in the attack.
