The Folk of the City

Yasmin

Yasmin is a married woman known for her taciturn nature. She engages in conversations with her neighbors about local events and gossip. Yasmin invited Gizem to the Spectral Hippodrome. at the Theodosius Obelisk, mentioning that while the horses are not real, the event remains entertaining. She established a meeting point for this event, demonstrating her familiarity with the area around the Hagia Sophia.

Yasmin communicates with her husband, Yusuf, through gestures and facial expressions, indicating a close relationship. She is actively involved in the local community and possesses knowledge of underground events, as evidenced by her invitation to the race, which attracts a diverse crowd. However, she has limited knowledge of the Undercity, particularly regarding its politics and operations.

Recently, Yasmin has become a social contact of Jean-Zera Mongé, who accompanied her to the spectral Hippodrome chariot races. During this event, Yasmin placed a bet of 15 silver on the Green Team while engaging with Chique Manu, a wealthy bookmaker. At the races, Chique Manu greeted her and Jean-Zera Mongé, acknowledging their taste in entertainment. Yasmin attempted to steer conversations away from financial discussions, showcasing her involvement in the social scene surrounding the races.
