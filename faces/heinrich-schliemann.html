Heinrich Schliemann

Heinrich Schliemann, a male member of the Schleierman couple, is deeply involved in the archaeological dig near Thymbra Farm. He presents himself in modern trousers and a waistcoat, forgoing a black or white tie, which lends him a more casual yet charming appearance, accentuated by his long, salt-and-pepper curly hair. Known for his pleasant demeanor and engaging conversation, Heinrich Schliemann carries a staff and a small dagger, suggesting he is ready for adventure while maintaining a conservative demeanor to avoid offending others.

During a dinner at the Calvert farmhouse, Heinrich Schliemann is introduced by Osman Hamdi Bay, who acknowledges his contributions at the excavation site over the past week or two, where he has primarily performed simple tasks like fetching water and attending to the crew's needs. His importance is further highlighted when Frank Calvert discreetly pays Rufus Dimitrious for smuggling logistics and requests Heinrich Schliemann to join him, signaling his role in the ongoing activities.

Heinrich Schliemann is driven by a determination to uncover the secrets of ancient Troy, possessing a notable physical presence that suggests capability and an imposing demeanor. He carries a satchel and equipment, indicating his preparedness for exploration. His close connection with Frank fuels their shared urgency regarding their discoveries. After initially being frozen alongside Frank due to a magical ward while investigating a leached tree, Heinrich Schliemann regains movement and expresses excitement about entering a door that could lead to ancient Troy, urging the group to act quickly despite potential dangers.

When a serpent unexpectedly drags him away, Heinrich Schliemann is taken by surprise and later emerges unconscious. Upon revival, he claims to have discovered ancient Troy, exhibiting a mix of pride and urgency, even as Frank and others advise caution. Ultimately, he agrees to defer further exploration, recognizing the necessity of a proper team to assist him, before heading back to the farmhouse with Frank, eager to share his findings.
