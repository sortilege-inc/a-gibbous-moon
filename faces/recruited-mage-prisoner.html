Recruited Mage Prisoner

powerful mage prisoner

The Recruited Mage Prisoner is a powerful spellcaster fitted with magic-dampening restraints that cover his mouth and hands. He was intended to be loosely restrained before an attack on Yedikule Fortress, allowing him to act in favor of Sublime Porte during the chaos.

During the assault, the mage cast a Cone of Cold spell, dealing significant damage to multiple attackers. After casting, he turned invisible and fled up a staircase, abandoning the defenders and escaping into the city.
