The Folk of the City

Usta Kerim

Dwarf Leader

Mr. Karen is a dwarf leader of a group of Child Gang members in the Undercity, marked by a rough demeanor and an authoritative presence. In a confrontation with Sidra al-Najjar, he threw a dagger, wounding her, and then engaged her in close combat using a scimitar, inflicting further injuries.

During the encounter in the alley, Mr. Karen issued commands in a Turkish dialect, asserting his control over the gang. He sustained injuries, including burns from Sidra's retaliatory magic and scorch marks on his beard. Ultimately, he was knocked unconscious by Gizem Faruki after a series of nonlethal strikes. Following his defeat, the gang members expressed uncertainty about their loyalty to him, indicating their youth and dependency. After the altercation, a heavy coin purse was found on Mr. Karen, containing 58 gold, which was decided to be shared among the children he had led.
