Elveda

The Elvish Woman is a member of a group of muggers involved in an ambush against the party in the Undercity. She appears youthful and possesses characteristic elvish features. During the encounter, she attempts to block the party's movement, indicating her role in the group's tactics. She is initially engaged in the conflict but is subdued by Ahmet Yusuf, who strikes her with a thorn-augmented quarterstaff. Following this attack, she cowers on the ground, remaining conscious but effectively out of the fight. A Ejder thug picks her up and retreats, suggesting a protective or strategic alliance between them within the group of muggers.
