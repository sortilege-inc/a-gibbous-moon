The Folk of the City

Elveda

The Elvish Woman is one of a band of footpads who set upon the party in the Undercity. She is youthful in look and bears the plain marks of her elven blood. In the fray she sought to bar the company's way, which was her office in the band's tactics; but she was soon overcome by Ahmet Yusuf, who struck her with a quarterstaff wreathed in thorns, after which she cowered upon the ground, conscious yet spent and out of the fight. An Ejder cut-throat then caught her up and bore her off in retreat — a sign of some protective or calculated bond between them within the band.
