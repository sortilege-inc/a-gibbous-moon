Medusa statue

Medusa

The Medusa statue is a large stone figure initially facing the wall in the cistern, magically linked to a Black Queen Chess Piece with a serpent. The chess-piece serpent detached, slithered into a keyhole on the statue, and completed an unlocking mechanism. The statue rotated to face outward; two pillars fell nearby, the water level rapidly drained, and a staircase appeared behind it. The statue spoke, "none shall free the snake demoness," and unpetrified. The Medusa fired arrows at range, wounding Jean-Zera; one arrow was caught by its petrifying gaze and turned to stone. The Medusa climbed down from its platform and attacked Gizem at close range with sword and snake-hair; the blades missed, but the hair bit Gizem and delivered heavy poison.
