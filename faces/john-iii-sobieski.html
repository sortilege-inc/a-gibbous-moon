The Folk of the City

John III Sobieski

John III Sobieski was the King of Poland and a celebrated victor at the Battle of Vienna against the Turks. A shrine dedicated to him features a pristine karabela Saber displayed in a glass case, highlighting his significance and valor in military history. The saber is in excellent condition, serving as a relic of Sobieski's legacy.

In a Private Library, a Malkoç Family Banner associated with Sobieski is discovered, further emphasizing his historical relevance. Artifacts found include a memoir attributed to Mehmed II, indicating the broader historical context in which Sobieski operated. These items signify Sobieski's impact on the region's history and his role as a prominent leader in the conflicts of his time.
