Mira

Mira is the sister of the sultan. She is distinct from the shamarin.

Mira is the sister of the Haseki Sultan Bergüzar Hatun and, like her, a powerful diviner. According to Osman Hamdi Bey's decrypted journal and The Shahmeran, Mira's very existence prevents her sister from reaching the full, Theodora-like power of a diviner: the nullifying effect grows stronger the nearer Mira draws, and while she lives, Haseki Sultan Bergüzar Hatun's power—and the Padishah's grand plan that depends on it—is checked. Osman Hamdi Bey kept Mira alive but hidden on a farm near the archaeological dig at Thymbra Farm, concealed her whereabouts within his journal, and erased even his own memory of the place. The party's recurring shared dreams proved to be a rebound of Mira's magic, a psychic bleed-over from her blood-borne struggle with her sister, caught when they first passed near her home. The Shahmeran urged the party to keep Mira alive—or bring her to the city to check Bergüzar—rather than see another Theodora rise. As the party set out to find her, Aziz Sefa Bey was already racing to reach and kill her first, ambushing them on the road near the dig.
