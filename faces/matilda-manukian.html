Matilda Manukian

Matilda

Matilda Manukian is a half-elf with access to the undercity. She serves as a fixer, facilitating connections and navigating the complexities of urban life. Matilda is involved in the underworld, providing safe passage and directions to the Leaky Pit, a vibrant arts district in the undercity.

During an encounter, Matilda tapped Sidra on the shoulder and acknowledged that the group had been seeking her. She offered assistance in escaping potential pursuers and led the group to a hidden staircase that provided a shortcut to the Leaky Pit. Matilda explained how to navigate the undercity and provided directions to a compound controlled by the Ghost of March, advising the group to announce themselves at the first building to request an audience. After delivering this information, she excused herself to attend to other business in the undercity.
