The Folk of the City

Matilda Manukian

Matilda

Matilda Manukian is a half-elf with the run of the undercity, who plies the trade of a fixer — brokering connexions and threading the intricacies of the city's life. She is bound up in the underworld, furnishing safe passage and directions to the Leaky Pit, a lively arts quarter below.

In one encounter Matilda tapped Sidra upon the shoulder and allowed that the group had been seeking her. She offered her aid in slipping any who might be in pursuit, and led them to a hidden stair that gave a shortcut to the Leaky Pit; she explained the ways of the undercity besides, and gave directions to the compound held by the Ghost of March, counselling them to announce themselves at the first building and there request an audience. That word delivered, she excused herself to attend to other business below.
