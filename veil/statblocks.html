Adversary Statblocks

☾
Keeper's numbers. Combat statistics for named adversaries, kept off the public pages (which carry only the narrative). These are reconstructed from play, not official blocks — treat them as a Keeper's working reference and adjust to your table.

The folk of the city wear their deeds in prose out front; their fighting numbers live here, where the players cannot see them. Add new adversaries to this page as they are statted, and keep the public Faces pages free of mechanics.

Hamit the Quartz of Damascus

Kavalan Pasha's foremost assassin — master of potions and firearms.

  • AC 17 (as mage armour) · HP ~90
  • Skills +13 Acrobatics, Sleight of Hand, Stealth
  • Weapon Thorn of the Dune — enchanted scimitar, barbed (+2 to hit and damage beyond ordinary steel); plus pistols, revolvers, hand-crossbow, and a tüfek (long Turkish musket)
  • Potion belt invisibility, speed (haste), flight, fire-breath, hill-giant strength, climbing, healing, poison resistance
  • Tactics strikes from concealment, drinks to escape or turn a fight; laid the midnight plot on the Vizier-i Aezam at Yedikule

Hamza

Barbarian frontline man of Hamit's crew.

  • STR 20 · HP high (a heavy soak)
  • Resistances piercing and slashing — even from magical weapons (while raging)
  • Weapon greataxe
  • Features Reckless Attack / Rage-frenzy — a brutal hand at close quarters, at the cost of taking heavy punishment; leans on potions to stay standing
  • Tactics kicks in the door and charges the van while his fellows fire from the rear

Note

Per house rule, all NPC/adversary combat statistics live here in The Veil, never on the public Faces pages. When a new adversary is statted from play, add a block above and keep their public page to narrative only.
