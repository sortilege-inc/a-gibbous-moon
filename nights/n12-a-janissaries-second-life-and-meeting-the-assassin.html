Night 12 · Session 12 · 2026-02-23

A Janissarie's Second Life, and meeting the Assassin

Summary

In the depths of the Undercity, Sidra al-Najjar, Jean-Zera Mongé, and Gizem Faruki strategized their mission to uncover the assassin of Kavalan of Egypt. They discussed using a Memory-Wipe Scroll after learning the assassin's scheme and held a fake copy of Osman's Journal intended for Aziz Sefa Bey. They noted the troubling absence of Janissaries who had defected with Alexander, indicating they had ventured into the catacombs and had not returned.

The group confirmed their entry point through the Basilica Cistern and began their descent into the shadowy depths. As they navigated the chamber, they discovered a freshly deceased young Janissary, his saber still at his side, and signs of a recent struggle nearby. Sidra used her thaumaturgy to force open a jammed door, revealing an old storeroom where a Giant Spider lunged from a chest, prompting a quick retreat.

Continuing their exploration, they found a steep stairwell leading to a corridor with an ominous, slightly ajar door. Gizem sensed unease emanating from the right, and as they advanced, Sidra spotted a human skull that appeared to shift unnaturally. Adjusting their formation, they approached a reinforced door, which Jean-Zera inspected before attempting entry. An arrow trap was triggered, striking Gizem as they opened the door, revealing a Human Skeleton lurking in the shadows.

The encounter escalated as Gizem grappled with the skeleton, tearing off its arm, while a desiccated undead humanoid emerged, speaking in an archaic Turkish dialect. It cursed Gizem, intensifying her suffering. Sidra invoked Sacred Flame, and Jean-Zera struck with Chill Touch, imposing disadvantage on the undead's next attack. With a coordinated effort, Gizem shattered the skeleton, scattering its bones, while Sidra unleashed a Fire Bolt that ignited the desiccated foe, reducing it to ash.

A living Janissary named Alexander appeared, warning the party to leave and insisting they had seen nothing. Gizem attempted to disarm him, but he resisted. Jean-Zera unnerved Alexander, allowing the party to subdue him without lethal force. In their conversation, Alexander revealed that Ahra 'the Wolf' had killed his wife, Nadia, for using magic. The party proposed that he mentor a young person struggling with uncontrollable magic, to which he agreed, provided they kept his location hidden.

Alexander confirmed that four defectors had entered the catacombs, some meeting grim fates, and the group discussed the need to prevent further undead manifestations. Sidra ventured further down the corridor, using Fire Bolt to incinerate additional corpses to thwart any potential reanimation. However, a massive skeleton emerged, towering over them and wielding a metal pipe as a club.

In a fierce battle, Miray Ruba Mata Biru channeled a radiant Moonbeam onto the Giant Skeleton, while Jean-Zera used Vicious Mockery and Faerie Fire to expose it. Gizem engaged in melee, attempting to weaken its structure. The Giant Skeleton retaliated, throwing bones to distract the party and swinging its club with devastating force. Despite a misfire from Alexander's weapon, the party pressed on, using teamwork to bring the creature down.

After the Giant Skeleton collapsed, the party collected its enormous skull as proof of their victory. Jean-Zera escorted Alexander to a safe location in the Undercity, where Papa Mert provided temporary lodging. Meanwhile, Miray delivered the giant skull to Hano, a bounty broker, who rewarded them with gold and a scroll containing two instances of Exorcise Djinni.

Gizem visited a bathhouse to discuss the influence of soft power in the realm, while Jean-Zera researched the Janissaries and the Sekban, uncovering the modernization shift under the Padishah's line. Through his family's network, he learned that Kavalan of Egypt was disgruntled and had sent agents ahead, leading to a meeting at The Pearl, an upscale brothel, to discuss the assassin's plans.

As they prepared for this meeting, Miray, scouting in fox form, spotted two well-dressed individuals tailing Jean-Zera. Inside The Pearl, Jean-Zera met with the assassin's representative, proposing aid through misdirection and compensation. The representative disclosed a decoy plot to leak information about a strike on Mustafa Pasha at Topkapi Palace, while keeping the real target hidden. To protect their interests, the party executed the Memory-Wipe Scroll, erasing the representative's recollection of their conversation.

The team took a long rest, readying themselves for the next phase of their mission in the Undercity.
