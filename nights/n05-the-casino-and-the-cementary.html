Night 5 · Session 5 · 2025-11-10

The Casino and the Cementary

Summary

The party made plans to split up for their investigations. Gizem Faruki and Jean-Zera Mongé ventured into a clandestine gambling club with Chique Manu, while Sidra al-Najjar and Ahmet Yusuf explored disturbances at a nearby cemetery. To blend into the gambling scene, Gizem adopted a male persona, styling herself as “Turkish Marlena Dietrich,” which helped her navigate the social dynamics of the club.

Upon arrival at the Hidden Tea-House Club, Gizem and Jean-Zera were greeted by an atmosphere rich with beaded partitions and lush decor. The clientele consisted mainly of cosmopolitan older men, and the pair quickly engaged in conversation, learning about Chique Manu's extensive travels and his current efforts to finance Janissaries. Meanwhile, Sidra and Ahmet entered the cemetery under the cover of darkness, attempting to maintain stealth. Their efforts were soon thwarted when Ahmet stumbled over a gravestone, causing a faint chittering sound to cease in the dark.

As they cautiously explored, Sidra spotted a small, lemur-eared silhouette on a headstone, smoking a hookah. When they called out in Turkish to share the hookah, they were surrounded by multiple high-pitched voices demanding “cups” and “candy.” Recognizing the creatures from folklore as Scavengers—mischievous fae known for causing minor trouble—Sidra offered pocket baklava and candies, which were eagerly snatched by the small creatures. Using her magic, Sidra conjured smoke to enhance the ambiance, while the Scavengers revealed that a witch had taken the tree they once lived in, prompting Sidra and Ahmet to agree to help restore their home.

Their stealthy approach to the witch's tree was abruptly interrupted when stones were hurled at them, knocking Sidra unconscious. Ahmet dragged her to safety and revived her with healing magic. Sidra retaliated by launching a Fire Bolt at the tree, igniting its upper branches and illuminating the area. They soon perceived an invisible, ape-like creature moving among the flames. Coordinating their efforts, Ahmet struck the creature, causing it to fall from the tree after being hit by a Sleep spell, ultimately leading to its demise.

With the immediate threat neutralized, Sidra and Ahmet returned to the Scavengers, assuring them that the tree was safe. The Scavengers celebrated their victory with wine from their hidden stash. Meanwhile, at the gambling club, Gizem learned the rules of backgammon while Jean-Zera engaged in a high-stakes card game, accepting a 200-gold stake from Rami Sharif. As the game progressed, tensions rose, culminating in a showdown where Jean-Zera revealed a full house, only to be outdone by Chique Manu's royal flush.

The atmosphere shifted dramatically when Rashad, a rival player, attempted to abduct Chique Manu during a lull in the game. A chaotic pursuit ensued, with Jean-Zera and Gizem commandeering a carriage to chase after the fleeing pair. Despite a risky maneuver that resulted in a crash and injury, Gizem engaged Rashad in melee, landing multiple strikes while Jean-Zera used magic to distract him. Ultimately, Gizem subdued Rashad, freeing Chique Manu and returning to the club to finish the game, where Jean-Zera ended the night with a net gain of 300 gold.

Back at the cemetery, Sidra and Ahmet delivered the creature's body to Hano 'the Widow,' who confirmed they had resolved the cemetery's evil spirit problem. In gratitude, she awarded them a Silver Yatagan, a set of Silver-Tipped Throwing Knives, and a total of 200 gold, which they divided among themselves. The group received an invitation from Chique Manu to celebrate the upcoming Festival of Candles following the Padishah’s coronation.

Before concluding their evening, the party secured access to the Undercity by purchasing Permanent-Entry Coins, ensuring easier access for future endeavors. They also stocked up on healing potions and explored the market for various magical items, noting potential purchases for the future. As they regrouped, they shared their stories from the night.

Moments

  • Surrounded by Scavengers for Hookah and Treats: As Sidra al-Najjar and Ahmet Yusuf approached a curious sight, they called out in Turkish, asking if they could share the hookah. Suddenly, multiple high-pitched voices clamored around them, demanding "cups" and "candy," revealing that they were surrounded by small Scavengers. One voice asked, "Are you with Invisible Attacker?" while another insisted, "Did you bring the candy?" In response, Sidra reached into her pockets, producing a piece of baklava and some candy, which the nearest scavenger eagerly accepted, stuffing them into its mouth. Amidst the excitement, Sidra conjured smoke with her magic, prompting cheers from the Scavengers as one of them attempted to balance the hookah on a rock.
  • High Stakes Showdown at the Table: As Gizem Faruki revealed her hand, she showcased a full house. Rashad then unveiled a broad straight. Chique Manu laid down a royal flush, causing stunned silence at the table. The players processed the implications of such a powerful combination, and the stakes of the game increased. With the cards laid bare, the dynamics of the game shifted.
  • Sidra's Fiery Assault on the Invisible Creature: Sidra al-Najjar unleashed a Fire Bolt, igniting the upper branches of a tree. Through the flames, she perceived the outline of a roughly five-foot-tall, spindly, ape-like invisible creature. Coordinating with Ahmet Yusuf, she guided him to strike the creature, which he did with a well-aimed rock that knocked it off balance. As the creature fell, Ahmet cast Sleep, causing it to succumb to unconsciousness and plummet to the ground, where it met its demise from the fall. Sidra quickly doused the flames before they could engulf the tree completely, ensuring the fire did not spread further. The area fell silent, the only sound being the crackling of the extinguished flames.
