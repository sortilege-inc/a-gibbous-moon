Night 14 · Session 14 · 2026-05-09

The Shades and the Sword

Summary

After the unsettling events at Topkapi Palace, where an Egyptian Kavalan executed an assassin and summoned the dead to speak, the party departed the court, their minds heavy with the implications of the encounter. The corpse, under the influence of Speak with Dead, revealed fragmented memories, including a description of Jean-Zera Mongé, hinting at deeper connections and threats. Seeking refuge, the group retreated to Gizem Faruki’s home to rest and regroup.

That night, each member of the party experienced vivid dreams laden with significance. Jean-Zera was haunted by the image of a square piece of paper bearing an ornate sigil, feeling an urgent need to reclaim it. Gizem's dream offered her a sense of relief as she was guided by a younger Aziz Sefa Bey to the Sublime Porte, while Sidra found herself in a coffeehouse, grappling with a magical warning as a vendor attempted to cheat her. Meanwhile, Miray encountered a runaway girl who received a similar piece of paper, suggesting a web of connections among their dreams.

In the morning, the party convened to share their experiences, and Jean-Zera and Miray sketched the sigil from memory, revealing a shared urgency. They decided to investigate Osman Hamdi Bey’s House and library, hoping to uncover more about the mysterious sigil and its implications. Upon arrival, they discovered the library had been selectively looted, with crucial texts and Osman’s genuine journal missing. Ashraf, the tortoise, expressed his discontent over the disturbances, hinting at the intruders’ familiarity with the party.

Miray discreetly acquired a small key from Ashraf while Sidra distracted him with flowers. Jean-Zera, investigating Osman’s desk, found a secret compartment containing a sealed letter. The letter, addressed to the party, implied Osman’s demise and bequeathed his manor to them, along with a journal that contained vital information about a powerful diviner. The letter also hinted at a key to her location, urging them to seek an ally for guidance.

Debating their next steps, the party resolved to approach Alemdar Mustafa Pasha of March as their ally. Jean-Zera located the Lodge of Byzantia’s address, noting their meeting coincided with the new moon. Sidra consulted her djinn, who recalled a ritual involving the Lodge, imparting secret gestures that could aid their quest. Their investigation led them to an Old Perfumery, where Sidra encountered Sharif, who tasked her with obtaining information about a powerful item from the races.

At the Spectral Hippodrome, Gizem met Kore, who shared insights about the crowd’s traditions, while Jean-Zera sought out Susanna, a fixer, to negotiate time with the faceless ghost. Amidst the chaos of the races, Sidra and Miray slipped into the stabling area, where they confronted a Blue Rider who revealed his desire for vengeance against Justinian. The rider’s shock at learning of the emperor’s death centuries ago prompted him to abandon the races, leaving behind a clue about the Crown of Sargon hidden beneath the Maiden Tower.

As the races were rescheduled, the party relayed the location of the Crown to a friendly cat and prepared to pursue it. They met with Alemdar Mustafa Pasha of March, who reacted sharply to the mention of The Shahmeran, a powerful entity bound beneath the Basilica Cistern. Alemdar Mustafa Pasha agreed to assist in confirming Osman’s fate and tasked the party with retrieving the sword of Yakub Agha, the weapon used in a recent assassination attempt.

Their quest took them to a Coastal Manor Garden, where they encountered shadowy, semi-corporeal Janissaries soldiers. The ensuing battle was fierce; Gizem engaged the nearest shadow, while Sidra called down radiant magic to aid her allies. Miray summoned a storm, striking the shadows with lightning, and Jean-Zera unleashed magical attacks from a distance. Despite their efforts, a shadow’s strength-draining assault incapacitated Gizem, but Miray quickly revived her. As the fight escalated, the commanding officer of the shadows appeared, wielding the very sword they sought.
