Night 11 · Session 11 · 2026-02-08

Spectral Horses, and Lost Memories

Summary

During the night, Sidra al-Najjar, Jean-Zera Mongé, Gizem Faruki, and Miray Ruba Mata Biru experienced a shared nightmare in a harsh, cold desert landscape filled with distant sounds of battle. Upon waking, they felt exhausted and parched. Despite their fatigue, they began to formulate plans for the day ahead.

Gizem and Jean-Zera received an invitation from Yasmin, a social contact with limited knowledge of the Undercity, to attend the chariot races at the Spectral Hippodrome. Sidra and Miray planned to infiltrate the event separately. As they made their way to the Hippodrome, Jean-Zera questioned Yasmin about her understanding of Undercity politics, discovering her knowledge was limited.

To aid their stealthy approach, Sidra retrieved a cloak designed to enhance her stealth and coordinated with Miray to blend into the crowd rather than purchasing tickets. As they entered, Sidra skillfully navigated through the throng of attendees, while Miray stayed close to avoid drawing attention. Sidra soon found herself separated and inadvertently locked in a back corridor near the stables, where she discovered pens containing spectral horses glowing in eerie colors.

While hiding in the shadows, Sidra observed an elven woman performing a necrotic debilitation on one of the spectral horses. She discreetly messaged Miray to direct her toward her location but advised her to hold position until the situation became clearer. In the stands, Jean-Zera and Yasmin were greeted by Chique Manu, a wealthy bookmaker, who engaged them in conversation about wagers. Yasmin placed a modest bet on the Green Team, while Jean-Zera created a distraction to pull Chique Manu away, allowing them to disengage.

Miray quietly opened the service door, spotted Sidra, and locked eyes with the elven saboteur, who fled deeper into the Hippodrome. From their vantage point, Jean-Zera and Miray witnessed the same elven woman whispering to Chique Manu during the race, indicating potential collusion. After the race concluded, Sidra searched for the backstage door she had used but found only an unbroken wall where it had been.

Jean-Zera examined the arena and detected a pervasive enchantment suffusing the entire structure, while the stonework itself remained material. He congratulated Chique Manu on his winnings and requested an introduction to the elven woman, identified as Susanna. The group regrouped after the race, sharing their findings about the elven sabotage and her connection to Chique Manu before dispersing to pursue further leads.

Gizem visited Cyrus, a gray-bearded dwarf druid and bead seller, seeking guidance on the Undercity while browsing ritual and magical items. Cyrus recommended the Styx Bathhouse for self-care and networking, mentioning the Misty Pond as a notable location. Meanwhile, Jean-Zera searched for records on the Hippodrome and race-fixing at Osman’s Library but found little useful material in Osman’s absence.

At the library, Ashraf, Osman’s rude tortoise, explained practical frameworks for fixing the spectral races, suggesting that layered intermediaries like Chique Manu could reduce exposure. Sidra checked on Shehrazat, a young girl with unstable magic, and discussed finding Alexander as a mentor. Gizem consulted Sif, a tiefling bookseller, who reported having recently sold an excellent volume to Chique Manu.

Miray visited the Styx Bathhouse, receiving an orientation from a hirsute half-elf attendant and observing the communal pool area. Hussein, the operator, explained how the venue was ideal for gathering rumors and conducting business. While there, Miray learned of a bounty on a selective-targeting undead threat near the city’s north side, with advice to negotiate with Hano 'the Widow' for better terms.

Sidra and Jean-Zera visited a Janissary hideout to inquire about Alexander, the recent defector recommended as a mentor for Shehrazat. A Janissary Lieutenant revealed that Alexander had ventured into a specified area of catacombs a couple of days prior and had not returned. As dusk approached, the trio arrived at the Dolmabahce New Palace Construction Site, guided by a servant of Osman Hamdi Bey.

Inside, they were introduced to the newly crowned Padishah Abd-ul Mejid and his wife, Haseki Sultan Bergüzar Hatun. Aziz Sefa Bey, a spectacled official, briefed the trio as assets cultivated by Osman. Haseki Sultan Bergüzar presented them with a silver box containing a scroll, which was to be used to erase any memory of their meeting from an assassin’s mind, preserving the integrity of a planned operation.

After departing, the group examined the scroll, inferring it functioned like a powerful, lasting memory-altering effect. Miray recognized the Sultan as the woman whose youthful face had appeared in their shared dreams, raising concerns about her potential influence. The group debated whether the dreams were planted by the Sultan or warnings from another source, deciding to keep a close watch on her actions.

Jean-Zera began planning how to discreetly move the fake journal to Aziz without implicating their allies. Meanwhile, Gizem sought racing tips from Kore, a singer affiliated with the Ghost, who reiterated her loyalty to the Blue Team without providing actionable insight. Miray scouted for the undead bounty’s general vicinity and opted to involve the entire party rather than risk a solo engagement.

That night, all four experienced another shared dream of a reduced Turkish military encampment where sparring and instruction were underway. They saw a youthful Padishah Abd-ul Mejid training under Alemdar Mustafa Pasha, who emphasized lessons about wounds bringing enlightenment. The dream took a turn when the youth lashed out with magic, prompting a rebuke from Alemdar. As the dreamers attempted to flee, they were snagged by a whip, and one of them attempted to cast a spell, which Alemdar countered.

The group aligned on their next steps: to meet Hano about the undead bounty, investigate the catacombs for Alexander, leverage the Styx Bathhouse for assassin intel, and coordinate with Aziz using the addresses provided.
