Night 10 · Session 10 · 2026-01-19

The Ciphered Journal

Summary

The party approached their meeting with the Ghost of March with caution, agreeing to keep their connection to Osman Hamdi Bey private. Upon entering the Undercity, they encountered a compound guarded by an orcish figure and a halfling in fatigues, who allowed them entry without challenge. Inside, they navigated through a series of open-air and enclosed rooms, observing strategy spaces and a training yard that suggested the group styled itself as a modernized army reminiscent of the Sekban. Gizem Faruki noted the presence of maps from Napoleonic and Egyptian theaters, while Jean-Zera Mongé speculated on the uniforms, which appeared either newly made or actual surplus.

As they moved deeper into the compound, Sidra al-Najjar detected the scent of gunpowder mingling with the aromas of fresh bread and stew, while her jinn signaled unease, prompting her to remain vigilant. They were soon greeted by Kore, a singer from Arraf Tavern, who led them to meet Alemdar Mustafa Pasha, the reputed dead commander of the Sekban. Alemdar acknowledged Gizem by name, indicating he was aware of their deeds, and inquired if they had been followed. When they expressed their purpose for visiting—retrieving something of Osman’s—Alemdar produced a leather-tied journal he had taken but could not decipher.

Jean-Zera examined the journal, identifying a complex cipher with magical components that would require significant skill to decode. Alemdar then requested their assistance in forming a united front with the Janissaries, specifically asking them to approach Ahra, the Wolf, despite their previous enmity. He spoke vaguely of a common adversary, emphasizing the need for unity among all occupants of the Empire. Gizem attempted to discern Alemdar's true nature, noting his bandaged appearance, which Sidra's Djinn indicated was magical, preserving him while protecting those around him.

After a meal in the cafeteria, where they overheard various dialects, the group discussed Alemdar's tactical demeanor and the implications of recent weapon caches being targeted. They noted the political climate was shifting with the new Padishah’s crowning, leading to a split plan: they would visit Shehrazat, a young tiefling suffering from “blood rot,” and seek Kore at Arraf Tavern, while also planning to attend the upcoming “specters” race.

Upon reaching Shehrazat's tenement, Sidra found her shivering beneath layers of blankets, her skin cracked and hardened. After providing her cloak, Jean-Zera cast Lesser Restoration to ease her condition. Shehrazat revealed her illness began after an involuntary surge of magic, worsening over two weeks. Miray used Healing Word and druidic rites, but a rebound of “Hellfire” scorched them all before dissipating. They proposed finding a tutor to help Shehrazat control her innate fire, agreeing that someone should stay with her while Sidra sought further aid.

At Arraf Tavern, they consulted Kore, who provided insights into Alemdar’s past and the political dynamics of the Undercity. She expressed her views on preserving Undercity spaces while ensuring safety and freedom of movement. After detecting a tail following them, the group split to divert and backtrack, with Sidra enlisting Papa Mert’s help for a remedy for Shehrazat’s condition.

After successfully applying the remedy, Shehrazat settled into a deep sleep, prompting the group to report their success to Alemdar. He approved their efforts and recommended they seek Alexander, a former colleague of his, for tutoring Shehrazat. Jean-Zera noted the library's focus on military works and planned to connect with other druids for additional support.

As they prepared for the next day, Osman handed Jean-Zera a gold-foiled invitation from Abd-ul Mejid for a meeting at the new palace construction site. The invitation hinted at an audience with His Imperial Majesty and referenced the Kavalan’s Rebellion. Jean-Zera executed a swap of the original journal for a fake, ensuring they could navigate the political landscape without detection. With plans to attend the specters race, check on Shehrazat, and pursue Cyrus, the group was poised for the challenges ahead.
