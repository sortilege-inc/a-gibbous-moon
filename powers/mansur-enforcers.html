Mansur enforcers

The Mansur enforcers are two guards set at the accessible entrance to the Basilica Cistern beneath Constantinople. They are Turks, and they keep the place off-limits by warning those who come to turn back. They caution that they have never themselves ventured beyond the entrance, remark that the nearby boats and raised boardwalks may not be trusted, and report having seen nothing in the waters but Bichura spirits.

When a company arrived at the cistern's mouth, the Mansur enforcers bade them turn back and told over the local hazards and their sightings of spirits. But after Jean-Zera spoke with them and put forward a false claim of official business, the enforcers took the story for true and suffered the company to pass.
