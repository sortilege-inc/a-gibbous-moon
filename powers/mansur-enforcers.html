Mansur enforcers

The Mansur enforcers are two guards stationed at the accessible entrance to the Basilica Cistern beneath Constantinople. They are described as Turks who attempt to keep the area off-limits by warning visitors to turn back. They caution that they have never ventured beyond the entrance, mention that the nearby boats and raised boardwalks may not be reliable, and report having only seen Bichura and Bichura spirits in the waters.

When a group arrived at the cistern entrance, the Mansur enforcers warned them to turn back and described local hazards and spirit sightings. After Jean-Zera spoke with them and presented a false claim of official business, the enforcers accepted the story and allowed the group to pass.
