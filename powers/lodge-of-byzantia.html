Lodge of Byzantia

The Lodge of Byzantia is a secret order focused on arcane knowledge and influence. Osman Hamdi Bay, a member of the Lodge, bequeathed his manor and its hidden compartments, including an artifact vault, to his friends. His journal contains the location of a powerful diviner, whose existence is said to block the full arcane potential of the Padishah’s Consort, thus preventing the Padishah's grand plan. The journal is secured with mundane and arcane locks, and Osman indicates that another way to access it involves seeking help from an ally.

The Lodge meets on the new moon. Sidra al-Najjar's djinn recalled being summoned by a Lodge ritual and imparted two secret gestures associated with the Lodge. Osman recently mentioned needing assistance from “The Shahmeran,” a powerful and dangerous entity bound beneath the Basilica Cistern since the time of Justinian and Theodora.
