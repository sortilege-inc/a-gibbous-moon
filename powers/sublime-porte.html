Sublime Porte

The Sublime Porte is a governing body within the Ottoman Empire, represented by the Padisha and his wife, the Sultan. They engage in political maneuvering and intelligence operations to maintain control and influence over the empire. The Sublime Porte has tasked a group of individuals, identified as assets cultivated by Osman Hamdi Bay, with capturing a Skilled Assassin sent by Kavalan Pasha. Their mission involves identifying the assassin's tactical position without disrupting the planned operation.

During a meeting, the Sultan presented a scroll designed to erase any memory of the trio's encounter from the assassin's mind, ensuring the integrity of their operation. The group was informed that the Sultan may possess formidable magical abilities, as she appeared in their shared dreams, indicating a connection to their current circumstances. The trio is to follow up on leads regarding the assassin and other matters while navigating the complex political landscape influenced by the Sublime Porte.

More recently, a group known as The Party has emerged, dedicated to protecting Mustafa Pasha, Vizier-i Aezam-i Azam, from an assassination plot by Hamit the Quartz of Damascus. They operate from Yedikule Fortress, where they set a trap to capture Hamit alive and erase his memory of their encounter using a memory-erasing scroll. The Party's strategic planning involved fortifying the fortress, recruiting allies, and employing various traps for defense. They spent approximately a day preparing Yedikule Fortress, implementing a decoy plan that disguised the real Vizier as an ordinary prisoner while placing a decoy in the Royal Cell. They withheld the true identity of the prisoner from the Mansur Enforcers to prevent leaks.

During the assault, Hamit and his crew engaged in combat, resulting in casualties among the hired guards. The Party's defenses included veteran fighters hidden as prisoners and traps such as a sleep gas grenade and a falling net. Despite their preparations, the attackers managed to clear several rooms and sustain injuries, leading to a chaotic confrontation. Ultimately, Hamit was captured, and his memory was erased before a diplomatic meeting where his death was orchestrated by Kavalan.
