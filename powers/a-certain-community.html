A Certain Community

A Certain Community operates as a clandestine service within the Empire, functioning as a secret police or intelligence agency. It comprises a network of spies and operatives who work outside the formal law enforcement structure, instilling concern among those aware of its existence. At the helm is Osman Hamdi Bey, a notable patron with the authority to deputize individuals, providing them with resources and discretion for their missions. The Community's A Certain Community is a state security apparatus led by a highly educated, multilingual chief who prioritizes the interests of humans within the Empire and opposes pluralism. They advocate for a singular, harmonious voice in governance and are particularly concerned about the influence of magical groups, willing to use magical means to suppress them. This faction views the current political climate as chaotic due to the presence of multiple voices, often criticizing Osman for his ecumenical pursuits.

In recent developments, Osman Hamdi Bey has tasked agents of A Certain Community with recovering his stolen journal. This mission is urgent due to the recent death of The Caliph, which has created a power vacuum requiring their involvement in the evolving political landscape. The agents are instructed to infiltrate the operations of various underworld figures, including Hano “the Widow,” Giannis the Cut “the Cut,” and Chique Manu, to gather intelligence and restore order. The A Certain Community is also investigating Osman and his dealings for potential corruption, making efforts to publicly associate Jean-Zera Mongé with Giannis to tarnish his reputation and undermine any future dealings he might have.

Additionally, in their first interaction with Ahra “the Wolf,” a leader of the Janissaries, the agents learn their cooperation with A Certain Community will involve not only recovering the journal but also engaging in criminal infiltration to help the Janissaries regain strength. The objectives of the agents are aligned with the broader goals of A Certain Community in maintaining stability within the Empire.

Beyond its secretive operations, the Certain Community also has a presence in the Undercity, where it focuses on local governance and the welfare of its inhabitants. This aspect of the organization is led by a figure known as "Ghost of March," who is perceived to hold legitimacy among the populace. The organization aims to maintain order, address crime-related issues, and support vulnerable groups, such as children influenced by local gangs.

Recently, members of the Certain Community, including youths under the control of a dwarf leader named Mr. Karen, were involved in a confrontation with a party of adventurers. The adventurers intervened to protect the children from threats, leading to conflicts that highlighted the dynamics of power and protection within the Undercity. After subduing Mr. Karen, the adventurers arranged for the children's safety with a contact named Kore, who promised them lodging and protection. Ghost of March's role in maintaining order indicates that while the Certain Community operates independently, it remains subject to the overarching authority of Ghost of March and his organization, further complicating the intricate web of influence that A Certain Community weaves throughout the Empire.
