Bostanji Imperial Guard

The Bostanji Imperial Guard serves as the military force of the realm, responsible for maintaining order and security. This modernized military unit has replaced the historically significant Janissaries, who were dismantled following internal conflicts. The guards are recognized for their ceremonial duties, exemplified during the coronation of Abd-ul Mejid, where they showcased disciplined formations, banners, and military attire in a grand parade. Their role encompasses enforcing law and order along with performing ceremonial functions.

During the coronation events, the Bostanji Imperial Guard strategically sectioned off parts of the street to maintain clear paths for the procession, facilitating crowd control and creating a festive atmosphere. They distributed small bags of coins to the public, which garnered mixed reactions from citizens, some expressing loyalty to the former Janissaries. The guards maintained strict discipline, responding to potential unrest by discreetly removing instigators from the crowd. Their presence reflects the political and social dynamics of the region, embodying the current ruling authority while managing the legacy of the past.

In more recent developments, the Bostanji Imperial Guard—also referred to as the Bostanji Imperial Guard—has faced significant challenges following the assassination of Nazif, a perfumist, during the Festival of Candles. In response to the chaos that erupted due to the actions of the Ghost of March and his followers, who donned the uniforms of the disbanded Sekban soldiers, the guards declared a state of emergency. They were present at the scene, attempting to disperse the crowd and restore order amid wider unrest.

The guards confirmed Nazif's death and managed the situation as additional forces arrived, highlighting their historical ties to the enforcement of law and order. Their actions during this tumultuous time underscored their ongoing role in the city's power dynamics, particularly as they faced challenges from the Ghost of March and public discontent regarding the recent violence. Compounding their difficulties, the theft of state weapons from armories, linked to underground forces, further complicated their efforts. The guards' presence was crucial in navigating these tensions, reflecting the complexities of their position within the realm's current political landscape.
