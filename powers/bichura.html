Bichura

Bichura are tiny, humanoid-like spirits composed of spell residue. They are weak, mischievous, and individually timid and benign, resembling pixies or sprites.

One Bichura attempted to pick Sidra's pocket and vanished before capture. Sidra offered baklava by hand and then placed it on the boards; the pastry lifted by itself and disappeared northward. Another Bichura hurled a rock that struck Merrow in the head. A Bichura created improvised projectiles that hurled rocks past the party, one of which hit Jean-Zera in the back of the head. A nearby hunter's trap was noted as having been intended for the Bichura, but they were too light to trigger it.
