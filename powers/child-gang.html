Child Gang

The Child Gang operates in the Undercity and is led by a dwarf known as Mr. Karen. The gang consists of youths, including an orc, a tiefling, a Ejder, and an Elvish Woman. Their activities primarily involve petty crime, as demonstrated by their ambush on a group of adventurers in a crowded alley, where they attempted to rob and intimidate the targets. The gang is characterized by its youthful members and the coercive leadership of Mr. Karen, who directs them in illicit activities.

In a confrontation with the adventurers, the gang surrounded the party and engaged in combat. Mr. Karen attacked one of the adventurers, while the orc and tiefling participated in the skirmish. The adventurers ultimately incapacitated Mr. Karen and interacted with the remaining gang members. Following the conflict, the youths expressed a desire to leave Mr. Karen's control, seeking a safer place to stay. The adventurers assisted them by arranging lodging and providing gold, helping the youths distance themselves from Mr. Karen's influence.
