Council of the Nine Lives

The Council of the Nine Lives is a faction operating within the Undercity, focused on protecting the vulnerable and maintaining order among various factions and gangs. It is associated with the figure known as the Ghost of March, perceived as a leader who upholds a sense of legitimacy in the area. The council has a network of informants and allies, including a talking cat named Greek Captain, who encourages others to seek the council's aid.

The council is interested in finding and supporting "interesting people," indicating a disposition toward recruitment or fostering connections with individuals who may contribute to their goals. The council's influence is significant, as evidenced by their ability to maintain order and manage criminal elements. The Ghost of March, the council's leader, is known for exacting justice and has a notable presence in the Undercity's governance.

Recently, the Council of the Nine Lives has become a more prominent force within the city's political landscape, recognized for its growing influence and authority. The Ghost of March has recently assumed control of undercity access responsibilities, previously held by Nazif, a perfumist. This shift in power was marked by the assassination of Nazif during the Festival of Candles, an event that has raised significant concerns among the populace and rival factions alike. Following Nazif's death, the Ghost of March and his followers, dressed in Sekban uniforms, exited the festival scene as guards declared a state of emergency. This incident has sparked discussions about potential resistance movements and suggests that the Council of the Nine Lives may be part of a broader underground network or power struggle within the city. Additionally, the council learned from Sharif the Strong Fur, who has assumed Nazif's duties, that Ghost of March had relieved Nazif of his responsibilities, indicating a direct link between the Council and the evolving dynamics of power in the Undercity.
