The New Sekban

The New Sekban is Alemdar's movement (a placeholder name — the faction proper is unnamed) operating within the Undercity, characterized by a modernized military structure reminiscent of the Sekban. Their headquarters is a compound featuring open-air and enclosed rooms, including strategy spaces and a training yard. The faction is known for its diverse recruitment, with a cafeteria populated by non-human uniformed recruits. Alemdar Mustafa Pasha, the bandaged leader reputedly the dead Sekban commander, oversees the faction and engages in strategic discussions regarding unity among various groups, including the Janissaries.

Alemdar has foreknowledge of arrivals and has praised efforts in bridging the worlds above and below. He has requested assistance in negotiating terms with Ahra 'the Wolf' of the Janissaries to form a united front, emphasizing the need for cooperation among all occupants of the Empire. Alemdar has invited referrals for recruits and those skilled in elements or alchemy to join the "new Sekban Army." He has advised maintaining the appearance of ongoing enmity between the Janissaries and the New Sekban to mislead others.

The faction's library focuses on military works from various nations. Alemdar has expressed interest in understanding the reasons behind the departure of a former colleague, Alexander, who left their ranks to work under Ahra. The New Sekban is tactical and emotionally detached, with Alemdar indicating a readiness for potential bloodshed while discussing the political climate and the need for strategic alliances.

Recently, the New Sekban has expanded its influence beyond military matters, with connections to the singer Cori, who is affiliated with the faction. This relationship has brought the faction into the realm of Hippodrome racing, where they have begun to gather tips and insights that could further enhance their standing within the Undercity.
