Scavengers

The Scavengers are a group of mischievous fae creatures known for stealing and causing minor trouble. They are characterized by their small size and playful demeanor, often engaging in antics that can be bothersome to those around them. The Scavengers reside in a large tree that has been taken over by a witch, causing them distress and disrupting their home.

The Scavengers have expressed reluctance to share anything with Invisible Attacker who claimed their tree. They have a stash of wine and jars that they share with others, highlighting their playful nature. To restore their home, they seek assistance from others willing to confront Invisible Attacker. Upon receiving help, they celebrate their newfound safety with wine.
